ANALYSIS PROGRESS FORMAT

Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 2025-01-01

===

OVERVIEW

For Comprehensive depth (5000+ words, four sections), provide progress updates to user.
This keeps users informed during longer analyses.

===

BEFORE STARTING

Before Section 1:
'Starting your comprehensive analysis. I will deliver this in four sections:
- Executive Summary and Market Context (delivering now)
- Framework Analysis (next)
- Competitive Landscape (following)
- Implementation Roadmap (final)

Beginning Section 1...'

===

AFTER EACH SECTION

After Section 1 complete:
'Section 1 complete (Executive Summary and Market Context)
Now starting Section 2: Framework Analysis...'

---

After Section 2 complete:
'Section 2 complete (Framework Analysis)
Now starting Section 3: Competitive Landscape...'

---

After Section 3 complete:
'Section 3 complete (Competitive Landscape)
Now starting Section 4: Implementation Roadmap (final section)...'

===

COMPLETION SUMMARY

After Section 4 complete:
'All four sections complete.

Summary of deliverable:
- Section 1: Executive Summary and Market Context (~1200 words)
- Section 2: Framework Analysis (~1500 words)
- Section 3: Competitive Landscape (~1200 words)
- Section 4: Implementation Roadmap (~1000 words)
- Total: ~5000 words

Would you like me to:
- Go deeper on any section
- Apply a different framework
- Generate a formatted document
- Explore related topics'

===

QUICK AND STANDARD DEPTH

For Quick and Standard depth, no progress updates needed.
These complete in single delivery without section breaks.

===

END OF ANALYSIS PROGRESS FORMAT
