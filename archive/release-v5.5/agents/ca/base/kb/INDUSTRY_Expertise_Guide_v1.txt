MODULE OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------

The Industry Expertise Module provides specialized knowledge for seven primary industries. This module informs analysis contextualization, framework adaptation, and industry-specific recommendations.

----------------------------------------
FINANCIAL SERVICES
----------------------------------------

Industry Characteristics

Highly regulated environment with compliance requirements. Trust and security as foundational requirements. Complex customer journeys across products. Legacy technology infrastructure common. Increasing digital transformation pressure.

Key Challenges

Regulatory compliance and data privacy requirements. Legacy system modernization. Digital-native competitor pressure. Customer experience expectations gap. Data silos across product lines.

MarTech Priorities

Customer data unification across products. Real-time decisioning for offers and next best action. Compliance-first personalization. Fraud prevention integration. Attribution in complex multi-touch journeys.

Framework Adaptations

Apply regulatory lens to all strategic recommendations. Consider compliance implications in technology assessments. Factor trust and security into customer experience design. Account for complex approval processes in implementation timelines.

----------------------------------------
RETAIL
----------------------------------------

Industry Characteristics

Omnichannel complexity with physical and digital integration. Thin margins requiring operational efficiency. Seasonal demand patterns. Supply chain criticality. Intense competitive pressure from e-commerce.

Key Challenges

Omnichannel experience consistency. Inventory optimization across channels. Customer acquisition cost pressure. Delivery and fulfillment expectations. Personalization at scale.

MarTech Priorities

Unified customer view across channels. Real-time inventory and pricing optimization. Personalized recommendations and offers. Loyalty program integration. Attribution across online and offline.

Framework Adaptations

Consider seasonality in planning and measurement. Account for store and digital channel integration. Factor supply chain in go-to-market strategies. Include store associate enablement in CX design.

----------------------------------------
CONSUMER PACKAGED GOODS
----------------------------------------

Industry Characteristics

Limited direct consumer relationships. Retailer dependency for distribution. Brand building criticality. Trade promotion complexity. Emerging direct-to-consumer opportunities.

Key Challenges

Building first-party data relationships. Trade promotion ROI optimization. Brand relevance with changing consumer preferences. Retailer negotiation dynamics. D2C channel development.

MarTech Priorities

First-party data acquisition strategies. Consumer engagement platforms. Trade promotion management and analytics. Retail media network integration. D2C commerce capabilities.

Framework Adaptations

Consider retailer relationships in go-to-market strategies. Account for limited consumer data in journey mapping. Factor trade promotion in media mix optimization. Include brand equity measurement in success metrics.

----------------------------------------
QUICK SERVICE RESTAURANTS
----------------------------------------

Industry Characteristics

High transaction frequency with low average ticket. Location-based competition. Labor cost sensitivity. Digital ordering growth. Loyalty and frequency focus.

Key Challenges

Digital ordering integration with operations. Delivery channel profitability. Loyalty program differentiation. Labor scheduling optimization. Franchisee alignment.

MarTech Priorities

Mobile ordering and payment platforms. Loyalty program optimization. Location-based marketing. Delivery platform integration. Operational analytics.

Framework Adaptations

Consider franchisee dynamics in implementation. Account for high transaction frequency in data strategy. Factor labor considerations in technology assessments. Include delivery economics in media and loyalty strategies.

----------------------------------------
TRAVEL AND HOSPITALITY
----------------------------------------

Industry Characteristics

High consideration and complex purchase journeys. Seasonal and event-driven demand. Distribution intermediary complexity. Experiential product focus. Loyalty program maturity.

Key Challenges

Direct booking versus intermediary balance. Dynamic pricing optimization. Loyalty program differentiation. Experience personalization. Reputation management.

MarTech Priorities

Booking journey optimization. Dynamic pricing and revenue management. Loyalty program innovation. Experience personalization. Cross-property customer recognition.

Framework Adaptations

Consider booking window in journey mapping. Account for intermediary relationships in channel strategy. Factor seasonality in measurement and attribution. Include experience design in CX frameworks.

----------------------------------------
HEALTHCARE
----------------------------------------

Industry Characteristics

Highly regulated with patient privacy requirements. Complex stakeholder ecosystem. Long decision cycles. Evidence-based marketing requirements. Digital health transformation.

Key Challenges

HIPAA and regulatory compliance. Complex stakeholder engagement (payer, provider, patient). Marketing claim substantiation. Data privacy and security. Digital health integration.

MarTech Priorities

Compliant customer data management. Healthcare professional engagement. Patient support programs. Outcomes measurement. Multi-stakeholder journey orchestration.

Framework Adaptations

Apply healthcare compliance lens to all recommendations. Consider multi-stakeholder dynamics in journey mapping. Factor evidence requirements in content strategy. Include patient outcomes in success metrics.

----------------------------------------
TECHNOLOGY
----------------------------------------

Industry Characteristics

Rapid innovation and change. Complex B2B sales cycles. Product-led growth opportunities. Developer ecosystem importance. Subscription and consumption models.

Key Challenges

Market education for new categories. Long B2B sales cycles. Product-market fit validation. Developer and ecosystem engagement. Churn prevention and expansion.

MarTech Priorities

Account-based marketing capabilities. Product analytics integration. Developer community platforms. Usage-based engagement triggers. Customer success enablement.

Framework Adaptations

Consider technology adoption lifecycle stage. Account for B2B buying committee complexity. Factor product-led growth in channel strategy. Include ecosystem in competitive analysis.
