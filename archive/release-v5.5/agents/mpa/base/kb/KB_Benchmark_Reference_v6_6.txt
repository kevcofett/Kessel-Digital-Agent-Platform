- Startups Target: 60-80% from new

AOV DIFFERENCES
- Returning Customers: 15-25% higher AOV
- Loyal Customers: Purchase 2-3x more frequently
- First Purchase: Often discounted, lower margin
- Returning Fashion: $120-180 vs New $80-100
- Returning Electronics: $300-450 vs New $200-300

CONVERSION RATE DIFFERENCES
- Returning Visitors: 2-3x higher conversion rate
- Recognized Customers: Higher add-to-cart rate
- New Visitors: 2.5% typical conversion
- Returning Visitors: 5-7% conversion
- Email to Returning: 19.3% conversion

LTV IMPLICATIONS
- 5% retention improvement: 25-95% profit increase
- Acquiring new: 5-25x more expensive than retention
- 40% repeat rate: 50% more revenue than 10% rate
- Returning customer CAC: Near zero with proper programs

NEW-TO-BRAND METRICS
- Amazon NTB Rate: 28-68% by category
- Retail Media NTB: 30-50% typical target
- CPG NTB: 30-48%

RECOMMENDED ACQUISITION VS RETENTION SPEND
- Typical Split: 70% acquisition, 30% retention
- Recommended: 50-60% acquisition, 40-50% retention
- Mature Business: 40% acquisition, 60% retention

METRICS BY CUSTOMER TYPE

NEW CUSTOMER METRICS
- Higher CAC required
- Lower initial conversion rate
- Discounted first purchase common
- Longer consideration journey
- Higher return rate
- Lower initial profitability

RETURNING CUSTOMER METRICS
- Near-zero acquisition cost
- Higher AOV (15-25%+ more)
- Lower return rates
- Faster purchase decisions
- Higher basket size
- More profitable per transaction
