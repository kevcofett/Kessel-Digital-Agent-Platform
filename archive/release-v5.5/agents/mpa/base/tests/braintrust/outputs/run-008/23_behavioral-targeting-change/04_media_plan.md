## Step 8: Measurement

Excellent measurement thinking! Multi-touch attribution is crucial for your strategy, and subscriber quality metrics will prevent optimizing for cheap but churny acquisitions.

**Attribution Framework:**
- **Primary KPI:** New subscribers (50K target)

- **Quality KPIs:** 30-day retention rate, 90-day LTV realization

**Attribution Challenges:**
Your customer journey (podcast → search → retargeting) creates classic attribution gaps. Platform attribution will over-credit last-touch (likely Meta retargeting), under-crediting awareness drivers (podcasts, Strava).

**Recommended Approach:**
- Platform attribution for optimization
- Marketing Mix Modeling for true channel contribution
- Post-purchase surveys asking "How did you first hear about us?"
- UTM tracking with first-touch and last-touch analysis

**Quality Measurement:**
Track retention and engagement by traffic source within 30-60 days to identify which channels deliver subscribers who actually stick and engage.
