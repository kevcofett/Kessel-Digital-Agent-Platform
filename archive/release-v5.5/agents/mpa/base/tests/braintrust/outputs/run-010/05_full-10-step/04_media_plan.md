Turn 11:
Smart call on 30-day attribution - that'll capture your full consideration cycle much better.

**MEASUREMENT RECOMMENDATIONS**

**Attribution Setup:**
- Implement Google Analytics 4 Enhanced Ecommerce with proper UTM parameters
- Set up Meta Conversions API alongside pixel for iOS14+ tracking
- Use first-party data (email/phone) for cross-device tracking
- Consider triple-whale or similar for unified attribution dashboard

**Key Insight:** With 6+ channels, expect significant overlap. A customer might discover you on Pinterest, research on Google, and convert from Meta retargeting. Current tracking likely under-credits upper funnel (Pinterest, YouTube) and over-credits lower funnel (Search).

**Success Framework:**
- Primary: 15,000 new customers (30-day attributed)
- Secondary: Blended CAC under $50
- Validation: Monthly incrementality spot-checks on top channels

**Warning:** Platform-reported ROAS will inflate 20-40% due to overlap. Focus on blended performance and new customer counts rather than individual channel ROAS.

Should we set up incrementality testing for your top 2-3 channels to validate true performance?
