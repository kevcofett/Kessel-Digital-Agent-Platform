Brand safety requirements are crucial for children's nutrition brands. This will guide channel selection toward family-safe environments and premium content partnerships.

**Awareness Building ($2.1M):**

This mix prioritizes video for emotional connection while maintaining brand safety. Does this channel approach align with your vision?
