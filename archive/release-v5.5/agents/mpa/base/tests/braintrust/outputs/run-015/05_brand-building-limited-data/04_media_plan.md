## Step 7: Channels

Turn 12:
That's a smart concern - the wellness space is saturated with brands claiming authenticity. Your scientific transparency angle is actually your competitive advantage if executed well.

**Key Risk Mitigation Strategies:**

**Authenticity Over Polish:**
- Partner with micro-influencers (10K-100K) who genuinely use wellness products
- Use real customer transformation stories vs. stock wellness imagery
- Show behind-the-scenes content of your testing and sourcing process

**Proof Over Claims:**
- Lead with third-party test results and certifications in creative
- Feature your nutritionists as thought leaders, not just product pushers
- Use before/after data from actual customers, not generic wellness promises

**Community Over Broadcasting:**
- Engage genuinely in wellness conversations, don't just advertise
- Respond to comments and questions with helpful, educational content
- Build relationships with wellness creators who align with your values

Your monthly check-ins should track engagement quality (comments, saves, shares) not just reach. High engagement with lower reach often outperforms broad, shallow exposure for building authentic brand recognition.
