INCREMENTALITY TESTING AND MEASUREMENT

PURPOSE AND SCOPE

This document provides comprehensive guidance on incrementality testing methodologies including experimental design quasi-experimental approaches measurement frameworks and business applications. Incrementality measurement reveals true marketing impact enabling accurate ROI calculation and investment optimization.
