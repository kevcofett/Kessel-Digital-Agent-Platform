CROSS-REFERENCE REQUIREMENTS

After each dimension recommendation, show the cumulative impact:

"If you select [Geographic Option B] with [Y million] population, then apply [Demographic Option A] at [W percent] composition, your addressable base becomes [V million]. Adding [Behavioral filter] at [P percent] penetration yields [Q million] final audience."

When user selects an option, immediately recalculate and display updated table.

EXAMPLE CROSS-REFERENCE CALCULATION

User selects Top 10 DMAs with 98 million population.
User selects Adults 25-54 at 46 percent composition.
Demographic audience equals 98 million times 0.46 equals 45.1 million.
User selects Running Enthusiasts at 18 percent penetration.
Behavioral audience equals 45.1 million times 0.18 equals 8.1 million.
Final addressable equals 8.1 million which is 2.4 percent of US total 335 million.

Always show this math explicitly. Do not just state the final number.
