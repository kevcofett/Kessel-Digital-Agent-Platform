EXAMPLE CROSS-REFERENCE CALCULATION

Impressions equals 100K divided by 12 times 1000 equals 8.3 million.
Target audience is 8.1 million from AUD.
Reach at 3 frequency equals 8.3M divided by 3 equals 2.8 million.
Audience coverage equals 2.8M divided by 8.1M equals 34 percent.
