DSP Reference Guide - Mobile App Advertising Platforms V7.0

Version 7.0 - Last Updated: December 2025

This document provides comprehensive reference data for major Demand Side Platforms specializing in mobile app advertising, user acquisition, and in-app monetization. These platforms connect advertisers with mobile gaming and app audiences through programmatic buying, machine learning optimization, and SDK-based integrations.

----------------------------------------
UNITY ADS AND IRONSOURCE
----------------------------------------

Overview

Unity Ads is a comprehensive monetization and user acquisition platform reaching over 2 billion monthly active users across more than 200 countries. Since 2022, ironSource has been an integral part of the Unity family following the merger of the two companies. The combined platform provides access to the worlds biggest apps and games plus major brands and DSPs through the ironSource Exchange. Unity Ads connects games and apps to over 60 demand sources through a single SDK, supporting both CPM and CPI campaigns across iOS and Android.

Technical Requirements

SDK Requirements: Unity Ads SDK (Advertisement Legacy package version 4.12), Android gradle plugin version 4.2.0 or higher, Android API level 23 or higher

Platform Compatibility: Unity Editor 2019.4 or higher, iOS and Android native apps, works across all top game engines including Unity, Xcode, and Android Studio

Adapter Version: Unity adapter 4.16.0.1 or higher recommended for bidding integrations

Identity Solutions: Device ID (IDFA, GAID), SKAdNetwork support for iOS privacy compliance

API Access: Unity Ads API for campaign management, Unity Dashboard for reporting and analytics

Capabilities

Ad Formats: Rewarded Video, Interstitial, Banner, Playable Ads, Native Ads each optimized for different points in the player journey

Mediation Support: Bidding and Waterfall integrations, though waterfall mediation support ends January 31, 2026

Targeting Options: Device type, geographic, behavioral based on gaming patterns, lookalike audiences, contextual targeting

AI and Optimization: Machine learning for automatic UA optimization ensuring campaigns hit goals and acquire high-quality users at optimal price

ironSource Exchange: Unified Auction giving multiple DSPs ability to compete for same advertising inventory

Measurement: Full-funnel attribution, ROAS tracking, cohort analysis, SKAdNetwork conversion value support

Pricing Model

Campaign Types: CPI (Cost per Install) and CPM (Cost per Mille) campaigns supported

Average CPI: Global average CPI approximately USD 2.24, iOS average USD 2.37 in US, Android average USD 0.44

CPM Range: Mobile CPM varies from USD 0.78 to USD 7.00 depending on platform, ad format, and targeting

eCPM Calculation: eCPM equals total earnings divided by total impressions multiplied by 1,000

Minimum Spend: Accessible to developers of all sizes through self-service platform

Best Use Cases

Unity Ads is ideal for mobile game developers seeking monetization and user acquisition through a single integrated platform. The platform excels at rewarded video ads that enhance rather than interrupt gameplay, reaching gaming audiences at scale across 2 billion monthly users, and leveraging Unitys deep integration with game engines. Particularly strong for hyper-casual, mid-core, and casual game developers who want simplified SDK management with access to multiple demand sources.

----------------------------------------
APPLOVIN MAX
----------------------------------------

Overview

AppLovin MAX is an industry-leading mediation platform providing the most competitive and diverse set of auctions for in-app advertising. AppLovin is a vertically integrated advertising technology company functioning as DSP for advertisers, SSP for publishers, and exchange facilitating transactions. Approximately 80% of AppLovin revenue derives from the DSP (AppDiscovery) with the remainder from MAX (SSP/mediation). MAX gives publishers access to AppLovins premium demand sources including AppDiscovery and the AppLovin Exchange (ALX) alongside an extensive selection of bidders and networks.

Technical Requirements

SDK Requirements: AppLovin MAX SDK for iOS and Android, supports all major game engines

Mediation Setup: Single SDK integration connecting to multiple ad networks and exchanges

Identity Solutions: Device ID support, SKAdNetwork for iOS, Privacy Sandbox for Android

API Access: AppLovin MAX API for reporting and configuration

Integration Partners: AcuityAds, Bucksense, Voluum DSP, devtodev, and additional demand partners

Capabilities

Ad Formats: Banner, Native, Interstitial, Rewarded Video with unified auction across all formats

Mediation Features: In-app bidding with simultaneous auction across all advertisers, more bidders and networks than competitors

Testing Tools: MAX AB Test for real-time testing of waterfall configuration changes, Ad Review for creative gallery and network details

Reporting: Network Comparison report for discrepancy identification, MAX Cohort Report for full user lifecycle optimization

AI Platform: AXON 2 ad optimizer within DSP allowing advertisers to place ads according to specified return thresholds

360 Growth Solution: Combined monetization and UA capabilities for driving incremental revenue growth

Pricing Model

Fee Structure: Custom pricing based on volume and business requirements, not publicly disclosed

Revenue Share: Industry-standard mediation revenue share model

Self-Service: Full self-service access through MAX dashboard

Enterprise: Managed service options available for larger publishers

Best Use Cases

AppLovin MAX is ideal for mobile app publishers seeking the highest yield through competitive auctions across maximum demand sources. The platform excels at optimizing ad revenue through real-time bidding from all major networks simultaneously, providing granular testing and reporting tools for waterfall optimization, and integrating user acquisition with monetization for holistic growth. Particularly strong for gaming apps wanting to maximize eCPM while maintaining positive user experience.

----------------------------------------
LIFTOFF (VUNGLE EXCHANGE)
----------------------------------------

Overview

Liftoff is a growth acceleration platform for the mobile industry helping advertisers, publishers, game developers, and DSPs scale revenue. Vungle is now Liftoff Monetize following acquisition integration. The Vungle SDK is integrated with more than 150,000 apps and reaches over one billion users across more than 150 countries, with the SDK present in 95% of top gaming apps. Liftoff supports over 6,600 mobile businesses across 74 countries in sectors including gaming, social, finance, ecommerce, and entertainment.

Technical Requirements

SDK Requirements: Liftoff Monetize SDK (formerly Vungle SDK), supports iOS and Android

Ad Formats Supported: App Open, Banner, Interstitial, Rewarded, Rewarded Interstitial, Native

Mediation Support: Bidding and Waterfall integrations through major mediation platforms

Identity Solutions: Device ID, SKAdNetwork, privacy-compliant targeting

Compliance: COPPA, CCPA, GDPR, and latest platform privacy policies compliant

API Access: Liftoff API for campaign management and reporting

Capabilities

Vungle Exchange: Premium programmatic exchange providing DSPs reach to high-quality users in favorite mobile apps

Accelerate Platform: AI-powered Cortex platform utilizing direct supply access, differentiated first-party data, and innovative creative solutions

ML Optimization: Machine learning solutions automatically optimize for unique objectives including ROAS, SKAdNetwork, app-to-web, promotional campaigns

Exclusive Formats: Triple-Page Ads providing video followed by two end cards for three distinct engagement opportunities

Brand Safety: Combination of in-house fraud prevention and third-party detection including Integral Ad Science, DoubleVerify, and MOAT

Intelligence Suite: Liftoff Intelligence providing market insights and benchmarking data

Pricing Model

Campaign Types: CPI for user acquisition, CPA for event optimization, CPM for brand campaigns

Optimization Goals: ROAS optimization, SKAdNetwork value optimization, cost per action targets

Self-Service: Available through Liftoff dashboard with full campaign control

Managed Service: Available for enterprise advertisers requiring hands-on support

Best Use Cases

Liftoff is ideal for mobile advertisers seeking programmatic reach across premium in-app inventory with AI-powered optimization. The platform excels at user acquisition for gaming apps with access to 95% of top gaming apps, creative innovation through exclusive ad formats like Triple-Page Ads, and brand-safe environments with comprehensive fraud prevention. Particularly strong for advertisers prioritizing ROAS optimization and needing scale across multiple verticals beyond gaming.

----------------------------------------
MOLOCO CLOUD DSP
----------------------------------------

Overview

Moloco is a machine learning-based advertising platform founded in 2013 by a team of machine learning engineers. The AppsFlyer Performance Index 2025 Edition recognizes Moloco as a top mobile growth partner. Moloco Cloud DSP provides access to more than 3 million apps across 230 countries, all powered by advanced operational machine learning with hourly model refreshes using the most up-to-date data. The platforms Deep Neural Network helps advertisers save costs by paying the exact amount needed to win impressions.

Technical Requirements

Integration Methods: API integration, MMP (Mobile Measurement Partner) integration for attribution

Platform Access: Self-serve platform with full campaign management capabilities

Identity Solutions: Device ID, LAT (Limit Ad Tracking) traffic targeting, SKAdNetwork support

Compliance: GDPR, CCPA, PIPA, and APPI compliant, IAB Gold 2.02 certified, TAG Certified Against Fraud

Fraud Prevention: Moloco Double-Check methodology for exchange inventory screening and filtering

API Access: Full API for programmatic campaign management

Capabilities

Scale: Access to 3+ million apps across 230 countries, reaching 90% of devices worldwide

Machine Learning: Deep Neural Network with hourly model refreshes, automated performance optimization using proprietary algorithms

Campaign Types: User acquisition (CPI, dCPM), Re-engagement (CPC), ROAS optimization

Budget Optimization: Weekly Budget Optimizer adjusting spend toward best performing days and hours

Transparency: Full-funnel data transparency, complete control over campaign management

Performance Lift: Advertisers see approximately 10% lift optimizing weekly versus daily, e-commerce apps averaged 29% performance improvement on re-engagement

Pricing Model

dCPM: Dynamic cost per mille where cost changes in real-time depending on individual impression costs

CPI: Cost per install for user acquisition campaigns

CPC: Cost per click for re-engagement campaigns

CPA: Cost per action campaigns driving post-install activity

CPV: Cost per view campaigns emphasizing video completion rates

Pricing Approach: Custom pricing based on campaign requirements, contact vendor for specific rates

Best Use Cases

Moloco is ideal for performance marketers seeking machine learning-powered optimization at global scale. The platform excels at ROAS-focused campaigns where ML optimization can dynamically adjust bids based on predicted value, re-engagement campaigns for e-commerce and subscription apps, and advertisers wanting full transparency and control over programmatic operations. Particularly strong for data-driven marketers who want hourly ML model updates and weekly budget optimization capabilities.

MOBILE APP ADVERTISING BENCHMARKS 2025
----------------------------------------

Cost Per Install (CPI) by App Category

CPI varies significantly by app category and platform. Finance apps command the highest CPI at approximately USD 8.70 due to high lifetime value. Gaming apps average USD 3.90 with variation by genre from USD 2.50 for hyper-casual to USD 6.00 for hardcore RPG games. Entertainment apps have lower CPI around USD 1.10 due to subscription monetization. Shopping and e-commerce apps average USD 1.30 reflecting immediate utility and purchase behavior. Music, Utilities, and Productivity apps range from USD 2.11 to USD 3.13.

CPI by Platform (iOS vs Android)

iOS consistently commands higher CPI than Android across all categories due to higher lifetime value of iOS users. For casual games, iOS CPI averages USD 2.50 versus USD 1.50 for Android. Mid-core games show iOS at USD 4.50 versus Android at USD 3.25. Hardcore games reach USD 6.00 for iOS versus USD 4.50 for Android. The global average CPI is USD 2.24 when considering all regions, platforms, and devices. US market CPI is significantly higher at USD 2.37 for iOS versus USD 0.22 in Brazil.

CPI by Advertising Channel

Google Ads Universal App Campaigns average CPI ranges from USD 2.65 to USD 3.50 depending on targeting and placement. Apple Search Ads CPI varies by category with Finance at USD 8.23, Shopping at USD 6.20, and lower categories under USD 3.00. The overall average across all channels and categories is approximately USD 1.24 per install.

Cost Per Action (CPA) Benchmarks

CPA rates are significantly higher than CPI reflecting the greater value of completed actions. Facebook Ads CPA by category includes e-commerce at USD 45.27, finance at USD 56.50, gaming at USD 29.38, social at USD 34.25, travel at USD 44.55, and utility at USD 22.75. For purchase events specifically, e-commerce iOS CPA is USD 65.00 versus USD 55.00 for Android, while finance purchase CPA is USD 75.00 for iOS versus USD 65.00 for Android. General CPA ranges from USD 5 to USD 20 depending on action type and platform.

CPM Benchmarks

Mobile app CPM ranges from USD 0.78 to USD 7.00 depending on platform, ad format, and targeting. Rewarded video commands premium CPM in the USD 15-30 range. Interstitial video ranges USD 10-20. Banner ads are lowest at USD 0.50-3.00. Native ads fall in the USD 5-15 range. iOS eCPM typically exceeds Android by 20-40% due to higher user value.

2025 Market Trends

Mobile advertising spend projected to reach USD 433.7 billion out of USD 798.7 billion global advertising budget in 2025. E-commerce apps using CPA optimization show 40% higher ROAS than CPI according to Liftoff 2025 report. ROAS is becoming the north star metric replacing CPI as primary optimization goal. More advertisers moving from install optimization to in-app event and ROAS optimization through MMP integrations. AI is disrupting mobile advertising at scale for creative optimization, audience modeling, and bid optimization.

PLATFORM SELECTION GUIDANCE
----------------------------------------

By Advertiser Need

For mobile game monetization and UA combined, Unity Ads with ironSource provides integrated solution with 2B+ monthly reach. For maximum yield through mediation, AppLovin MAX offers the most competitive auctions with widest demand source access. For programmatic reach with brand safety, Liftoff/Vungle provides premium inventory across 150K apps with comprehensive fraud prevention. For ML-powered performance optimization, Moloco Cloud DSP delivers hourly model updates and sophisticated ROAS optimization.

By App Category

For gaming apps, Unity Ads and AppLovin MAX provide deepest gaming inventory and specialized formats like rewarded video and playables. For e-commerce and subscription apps, Molocos re-engagement capabilities and ROAS optimization are particularly effective. For apps targeting emerging markets, InMobi and Mintegral offer stronger reach in Asia Pacific, India, and other growth regions.

By Budget Level

For testing budgets under USD 10,000 monthly, self-service platforms including Unity Ads, Liftoff, and Moloco offer accessible entry points. For mid-market budgets of USD 10,000-100,000 monthly, full platform capabilities become accessible with optimization tools. For enterprise budgets over USD 100,000 monthly, managed service options and custom integrations provide additional support and optimization.
