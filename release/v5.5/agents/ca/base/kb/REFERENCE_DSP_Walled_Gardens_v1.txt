DSP Reference: Walled Garden Platforms V7.0

Version 7.0 - Last Updated: December 2025

This document provides comprehensive reference data for major walled garden advertising platforms including Meta, Google, TikTok, Snapchat, and Pinterest. Walled gardens are closed advertising ecosystems where the platform controls inventory, data, and measurement. These platforms collectively represent the majority of digital advertising spend and offer unique targeting, creative, and measurement capabilities not available through open programmatic exchanges.

----------------------------------------
META (FACEBOOK AND INSTAGRAM)
----------------------------------------

Platform Overview

Meta operates the largest social advertising platform globally, encompassing Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, and the Audience Network. The platform has shifted heavily toward AI-driven automation through its Advantage+ suite, which now powers 65% of US advertiser campaigns. Metas approach increasingly relies on algorithmic optimization rather than manual targeting controls.

Monthly Active Users: Over 3 billion across Meta family of apps

Primary Platforms: Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, Audience Network

Ad Manager: Meta Ads Manager (unified platform for all properties)

Advantage+ Automation Suite

Advantage+ is Metas AI-driven campaign automation covering targeting, placements, creative mix, and budget distribution. The platform uses machine learning to test multiple audience and creative combinations in real time, automatically allocating spend to top performers.

Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns: Automated shopping campaigns with 22% average ROAS improvement (Meta Q2 2025)

Advantage+ App Campaigns: Automated app install and engagement optimization

Advantage+ Lead Campaigns: Automated lead generation with dynamic creative

Dynamic Media Default: Enabled by default for Advantage+ Catalog ads as of September 2025

Targeting Capabilities

Meta eliminated detailed targeting exclusions in January 2025, citing 22% better performance without the feature. The platform now emphasizes broader targeting with algorithmic optimization rather than manual micro-targeting.

Core Audiences: Demographics (age, gender, location), interests, behaviors

Custom Audiences: Website visitors, customer lists, app users, engagement audiences

Lookalike Audiences: 1-10% similarity to source audiences, value-based lookalikes

Broad Targeting: Let Meta AI find audiences based on creative and conversion signals

2025 Changes: Detailed targeting exclusions retired, focus on first-party data signals

Ad Formats

Image Ads: Single image in Feed, Stories, Reels placements

Video Ads: In-feed video, Stories video, Reels video, in-stream

Carousel Ads: 2-10 scrollable images or videos

Collection Ads: Instant Experience with product catalog

Lead Ads: Native lead forms without leaving platform

Dynamic Ads: Automatically generated from product catalog

Reels Ads: Full-screen vertical video in Reels feed

Pricing and Minimums

Budget Types: Daily budget (consistent spend) or Lifetime budget (campaign total)

Minimum Budget: No hard minimum, recommended USD 10-50/day per ad set for reliable data

Average CPM: USD 7-15 for typical campaigns, USD 4 possible with Advantage+ broad targeting

Lead Gen CPM: USD 20-45 for lead generation campaigns

Image vs Video CPM: Image ads approximately USD 5 CPM, video approximately USD 7 CPM

Q4 Seasonality: CPCs increase 30-35%, CPMs surge 25-66% during holiday season

ROAS Benchmark: Image ads 159% ROAS, video ads 144% ROAS (2025 data)

----------------------------------------
GOOGLE ADS
----------------------------------------

Platform Overview

Google Ads is the largest digital advertising platform globally, spanning Search, Display, YouTube, Shopping, Gmail, Discover, and Maps. The platform has evolved toward AI-driven automation through Performance Max campaigns while maintaining traditional campaign types for advertisers requiring more control.

Search Market Share: Approximately 90% of global search

YouTube Reach: Over 2 billion logged-in monthly users

Display Network Reach: Over 90% of internet users globally

Campaign Types: Search, Display, Shopping, Video, App, Performance Max, Demand Gen

Performance Max Campaigns

Performance Max uses Google AI to optimize ad placements across all Google channels from a single campaign. The platform distributes budget across Search, Display, YouTube, Shopping, Discover, Gmail, and Maps based on predicted return. Google launched over 90 quality improvements in 2025, increasing conversions by more than 10%.

Channel Coverage: Search, Display, YouTube, Shopping, Gmail, Discover, Maps

2025 Updates: Campaign-level negative keywords, high-value customer mode, age demographic exclusions, device targeting

Brand Exclusions: Can now apply to Search text ads only while keeping Shopping branded traffic

Third-Party Safety: DoubleVerify, IAS, and Zefr exclusion lists available

Targeting Capabilities

Search Targeting: Keywords (broad, phrase, exact match), dynamic search ads

Audience Segments: In-market, affinity, custom intent, remarketing

Demographics: Age, gender, household income, parental status

Geographic: Country, region, city, radius, postal code targeting

Device Targeting: Desktop, mobile, tablet with bid adjustments

Customer Match: First-party data upload for targeting and exclusions

Ad Formats by Channel

Search Ads: Responsive search ads with up to 15 headlines and 4 descriptions

Display Ads: Responsive display ads, uploaded image ads, HTML5

YouTube Ads: Skippable in-stream, non-skippable, bumper (6 sec), in-feed, Shorts

Shopping Ads: Product Shopping ads, Performance Max for retail

Demand Gen: Cross-platform (YouTube, Discover, Gmail) with AI creative

Pricing and Minimums

Search Billing: CPC (cost per click)

Display Billing: CPC or CPM depending on campaign settings

YouTube In-Stream: CPM billing

Performance Max Minimum: Recommended USD 1,000-2,000/month for effective learning

Search Campaigns: Can perform well with smaller budgets when properly optimized

Average Search CPC: USD 1-4 depending on industry (legal and insurance highest)

Display CPM: USD 0.50-3.00 for standard placements

YouTube CPM: USD 10-30 for in-stream video

TIKTOK
----------------------------------------

Platform Overview

TikTok is the fastest-growing social platform for advertising, known for short-form vertical video and high engagement rates among younger demographics. The platform emphasizes authentic, creator-style content over polished advertising, with Spark Ads enabling promotion of organic posts.

Monthly Active Users: Over 1 billion globally

Primary Demographics: Strong Gen Z and Millennial audience, expanding to older demographics

Content Format: Short-form vertical video (15 sec to 10 min)

Ad Manager: TikTok Ads Manager

Ad Formats

In-Feed Ads: Native video ads appearing in For You feed, USD 4-8 CPM

Spark Ads: Promote organic TikTok posts (yours or creator content with authorization), 30-40% lower CPC than standard ads

TopView Ads: First ad users see when opening app, USD 10-20 CPM, starts at USD 50,000/day

Brand Takeover: Full-screen ads on app open, USD 20-50 CPM, starts at USD 50,000/day

Branded Hashtag Challenge: Sponsored challenges encouraging user participation, fixed fee

Branded Effects: Custom AR filters and stickers

TikTok Shop Integration: Spark Ads with product tags for shoppable content

Targeting Capabilities

Demographics: Age, gender, location, language

Interests: TikTok-defined interest categories based on engagement

Behaviors: Video interactions, creator interactions, hashtag interactions

Custom Audiences: Customer file, website traffic, app activity, engagement

Lookalike Audiences: Similar users based on custom audience seed

Automatic Targeting: TikTok AI finds relevant audiences

Pricing and Minimums

Campaign Minimum: USD 500 total

Daily Campaign Minimum: USD 50

Ad Group Daily Minimum: USD 20

Lifetime Budget Calculation: USD 20 x scheduled days (e.g., 31 days = USD 620 minimum)

Spark Ads Minimum: USD 20

Average CPM: USD 4-10 (official minimum bid USD 10, actual range USD 4.20-9.00)

Average CPC: USD 0.50-1.00

Spark Ads CPM: USD 1-4

Spark Ads CPC: USD 0.10-0.30

Q4 Increase: CPMs rise 20-40% October-December

SNAPCHAT
----------------------------------------

Platform Overview

Snapchat is a visual messaging platform known for ephemeral content, augmented reality features, and strong Gen Z audience. The platform offers unique AR advertising capabilities through sponsored lenses and filters, with AI-powered features expanding creative possibilities.

Daily Active Users: Over 400 million

Primary Demographics: Strong Gen Z and young Millennial audience (13-34)

Content Format: Ephemeral photos/videos, Stories, Spotlight (TikTok competitor)

AR Capabilities: Industry-leading augmented reality lens technology

Ad Formats

Snap Ads: Full-screen vertical video/image ads between Stories or in Discover

Story Ads: Branded tile in Discover section opening to 3-20 Snap collection

Collection Ads: Product catalog with tappable tiles

Dynamic Ads: Automatically generated from product feed

Commercials: Non-skippable 6-second video ads in premium content

Face Lenses: AR filters applied to users face, can include try-on experiences

World Lenses: AR objects placed in real world through camera

Sponsored AI Lenses: Generative AI-powered personalized AR experiences (launched 2025)

Filters: Branded overlays users can apply to their snaps

Targeting Capabilities

Demographics: Age, gender, location, language

Interests: Snapchat Lifestyle Categories based on content engagement

Behaviors: In-app activity, purchase behavior

Custom Audiences: Customer lists, website visitors, app users

Lookalike Audiences: Expand reach based on seed audience characteristics

Smart Targeting: ML-based targeting delivering 8.8% average conversion increase

State Targeted First Story: Geographic targeting for AR products (new 2025)

Advanced Data: Browsing history, location, app usage for refinement

Pricing and Minimums

Minimum Daily Budget: USD 5/day

Recommended Test Budget: USD 50-100/week for meaningful data

Average CPM: USD 9.63

Average CPC: USD 1.02

Average CTR: 0.94%

AR Lens CPM: Premium pricing, significantly higher than standard ads

Q4 CPM Peak: Up to USD 12.84 due to AR ad demand and limited inventory

First Lens Unlimited: 25-45% higher impressions with state targeting

Auction System: Bid amount, ad quality, and relevance determine placement

PINTEREST
----------------------------------------

Platform Overview

Pinterest is a visual discovery platform where users actively search for ideas, products, and inspiration. Unlike other social platforms, Pinterest users have high purchase intent, making it valuable for retail, home, fashion, and lifestyle advertisers. The platform has evolved into a full performance marketing platform with strong shopping capabilities.

Monthly Active Users: Over 450 million

Primary Use Case: Discovery and planning for purchases, projects, events

User Intent: High commercial intent, users actively seeking products and ideas

Key Verticals: Home decor, fashion, beauty, food, DIY, weddings, travel

Ad Formats

Standard Pins: Single image promoted in home feed and search

Video Pins: Standard video, max-width video, mobile-optimized video

Idea Ads: Multi-page content with direct links (enhanced 2025)

Carousel Ads: 2-5 swipeable images

Shopping Ads: Dynamic pricing and stock info from product feed, 15% higher ROI than standard pins

Collection Ads: Hero image/video with product grid below

AR Try On Ads: Virtual product try-on for beauty, eyewear, home decor

Shop the Look: Tap to shop products within lifestyle imagery

Targeting Capabilities

Keywords: Target based on search terms users enter

Interests: Pinterest-defined interest categories

Demographics: Age, gender, location, language

Actalike Audiences: Pinterests version of lookalikes based on user behavior

Customer Lists: Upload customer data for targeting

Website Visitors: Retarget users who visited your site

Engagement Audiences: Users who engaged with your pins

Shopping Audience Targeting: Pinterest matches product data to relevant audiences

Pricing and Minimums

Minimum Daily Budget: No official minimum, recommended USD 5-10/day

Recommended Test Budget: USD 50-100/week for sufficient data

Recommended for AI Optimization: Daily budget 5x target cost per result

Average CPM: USD 2-7

Average CPC: USD 0.10-1.50

Shopping Ads CPC: USD 0.50-2.00

Average CPA: USD 5-30 depending on conversion type

Bidding Options: Manual or Performance+ (AI-optimized)

Cost Efficiency: 2.3x lower cost per conversion vs other platforms

Audience Cost Factors: Broad audiences cheaper, narrow B2B targeting more expensive
