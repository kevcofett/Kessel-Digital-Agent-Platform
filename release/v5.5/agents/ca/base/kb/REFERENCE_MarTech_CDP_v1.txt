MarTech Reference Guide - Customer Data Platforms V7.0

Version 7.0 - Last Updated: December 2025

This document provides comprehensive reference data for leading Customer Data Platforms (CDPs). CDPs unify customer data from multiple sources to create persistent, unified customer profiles that power personalization, activation, and analytics across marketing and advertising channels.

----------------------------------------
TWILIO SEGMENT
----------------------------------------

Overview

Twilio Segment is the worlds leading Customer Data Platform, trusted by over 25,000 companies. The platform provides a data foundation for companies to put customers at the heart of every decision. Segments flexible, vendor-agnostic architecture scales seamlessly across all business sizes with high-capacity event streaming enabling management and activation of large data volumes in real time without latency. The platform originated as a data pipeline tool, making it particularly strong in developer-friendly APIs and rapid deployment.

Technical Requirements

Integration Options: 550+ pre-built destination connections including email, messaging, advertising platforms, and data warehouses

Data Collection: JavaScript SDK, mobile SDKs (iOS, Android), server-side libraries, cloud source connectors

Data Warehouse Support: Native integration with Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks for warehouse-centric workflows

Reverse ETL: Native Reverse ETL product validating data quality and activating warehouse data

API Access: Developer-friendly RESTful APIs for all platform functions

Identity Resolution: Cross-device identity stitching with visitor and user association

Capabilities

Connections: Customer Data Pipeline collecting first-party data and activating across 550+ destinations for unified customer view

Unify: Combines real-time events with warehouse data to build unified profiles enabling any team to use trusted data

Engage: Complete real-time CDP enabling technical and business users to build and activate customer profiles and audiences

CustomerAI: AI-powered product recommendations, traits, and predictions designed to drive conversions through targeted audiences

Predictive Traits: Machine learning anticipating customer behavior with 57% year-over-year adoption growth

Data Quality: Intuitive data cleaning features and real-time validation built for enterprise scale

Protocols: Data governance and schema management included in all CDP plans

Pricing Model

Pricing Basis: Monthly Tracked Users (MTU) calculated as monthly active users plus anonymous visitors

CDP Plans: Unify and Twilio Engage as primary CDP plans with Protocols included

Free Trial: 14-day free trial for Connections Team plan, no free trial for CDP plans

Enterprise Discounts: Renewal negotiations offer 10-15% improvement, with enterprise deals achieving 85-90% discounts off list price

Self-Service: Connections plans available self-service, CDP plans require sales engagement

Best Use Cases

Twilio Segment is ideal for organizations with strong engineering teams seeking rapid implementation and developer-friendly tools. The platform excels at proof-of-concept deployments completing in days rather than months, warehouse-centric architectures leveraging Snowflake or BigQuery, and extensive destination activation across 550+ marketing and analytics tools. Particularly strong for digital-first companies, SaaS businesses, and organizations prioritizing data engineering integration patterns.

----------------------------------------
TEALIUM
----------------------------------------

Overview

Tealium has been recognized as a Leader in the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Customer Data Platforms for two consecutive years. The platform originated in tag management and evolved into a comprehensive Customer Data Hub. Tealium serves mid-market and enterprise clients with particular strength in retail and banking sectors. The marketer-friendly interface enables no-code audience building and enrichment rules, differentiating from more developer-centric alternatives.

Technical Requirements

Integration Options: 1,300+ pre-built integrations through Integrations Marketplace

Data Collection: Tag management (Tealium iQ), server-side tracking, mobile SDKs, API connectors

Data Storage: Customer Data Hub for data management and storage with embedded analytics

Cloud Support: CloudStream for activating customer data directly from data cloud with zero duplication

AI Platform: Tealium AIStream as intelligent real-time customer data stream for AI applications

Identity Resolution: Patented visitor stitching technology constructing comprehensive customer profiles

Capabilities

AudienceStream CDP: Real-time CDP with predictive insights, native audience building, and real-time enrichment

Tag Management: Enterprise-grade tag management through Tealium iQ

Tealium Moments: Real-time intelligence and action for owning customer experience moments

Predictive ML: Tealium Predict ML for anticipating customer behavior and next best actions

Data Quality: Real-time data collection and quality assurance

Data Governance: Enterprise data orchestration with compliance controls

Reporting: Embedded analytics for reporting and dashboarding

Pricing Model

Pricing Basis: Event volume with included service hours based on volume tier

Bundle Discounts: Available for solution bundles including full Tealium product suite

Cost Positioning: More affordable than Adobe without suite lock-in, but higher cost may limit smaller businesses

Enterprise Focus: Less cost-effective for smaller organizations, pricing scales with data volume

Quote Required: Contact Tealium for custom pricing based on volume

Best Use Cases

Tealium is ideal for marketing teams seeking no-code audience building with comprehensive tag management heritage. The platform excels at web-first organizations with complex tag management needs, enterprise clients in retail and banking requiring real-time enrichment, and companies wanting 1,300+ integrations without heavy developer involvement. Particularly strong for mid-market to enterprise organizations transitioning from tag management to full CDP capabilities.

----------------------------------------
MPARTICLE
----------------------------------------

Overview

mParticle is a customer data platform now part of Rokt, known for strength in mobile app ecosystems and product-focused teams. The platform serves major brands including HBO Max, Marks & Spencer, JetBlue, and SoFi. mParticle positions itself as the hybrid CDP combining real-time responsiveness with warehouse-native scale. The platform originated in mobile SDK integration and is used by app-first businesses including Spotify and Airbnb.

Technical Requirements

Integration Options: 300+ API-based integrations for seamless data flow across systems

Data Collection: Best-in-class mobile SDKs, web SDK, server-side integrations

Data Warehouse: Streaming to Data Warehouses forwarding raw events to Snowflake or BigQuery

Hybrid Architecture: Combines real-time responsiveness with warehouse-native scale

Developer Tools: Robust APIs, command-line interface, advanced developer resources

Snowflake Partnership: Hybrid CDP on Snowflake AI Data Cloud launched 2025

Capabilities

Core Domains: Data Connections, Data Quality, Data Governance, Data-Driven Personalization

Audience Engine: Most powerful and flexible audience engine in CDP category with real-time and long-term lookback, nested audiences

AI Predictions: AI-powered customer insights including likelihood to purchase and churn risk

Lookalike Audiences: Reach new users sharing characteristics with existing customers

Mobile Excellence: Industry-leading mobile SDK integration for app-first businesses

Event Tiering: Ephemeral, stored, and real-time event designations for cost optimization

Pricing Model

Model: Value-based pricing with universal credits and usage-based structure

Credit System: Universal credits usable on any product or service with no feature-gating

Consumption-Based: Pay only for what you use with clear usage metrics

AI Access: Unfiltered access to AI, analytics, and unlimited Audiences included

Event Optimization: Event tiering options reduce storage and compute costs for ephemeral data

Quote Required: Contact mParticle for custom enterprise pricing

Best Use Cases

mParticle is ideal for mobile-first businesses and product teams seeking best-in-class mobile SDK integration. The platform excels at app-first companies in entertainment, transportation, and media industries, technical teams wanting raw data access for machine learning and SQL analysis, and organizations requiring flexible hybrid architectures combining real-time and warehouse-native approaches. Particularly strong for Snowflake customers through Hybrid CDP partnership.

ADOBE REAL-TIME CDP
----------------------------------------

Overview

Adobe Real-Time CDP is built on Adobe Experience Platform and provides unified, real-time customer profiles from known and anonymous data. The platform offers B2C, B2B, and B2P (Business-to-Person) editions for different market needs. Adobe Real-Time CDP integrates natively with Adobe Experience Cloud including Adobe Journey Optimizer, Adobe Target, and Adobe Analytics. Adobe Sensei AI powers actionable insights and predictive analytics throughout the platform.

Technical Requirements

Platform Foundation: Built on Adobe Experience Platform with unified XDM (Experience Data Model) schema

Adobe Integration: Native integration with Adobe Journey Optimizer, Target, Analytics, Campaign

Partner Ecosystem: Large ecosystem of non-Adobe partners for activation beyond Adobe products

Data Governance: Enterprise data governance including consent collection, data labeling, and policy enforcement

AI Platform: Adobe Sensei AI for insights, predictions, and automated optimization

Identity Resolution: Cross-channel identity resolution linking known and anonymous customer data

Capabilities

Real-Time Profiles: Unified customer profiles updating in real time from multiple sources

Segmentation: AI and rule-based intelligent segments updating in real time

Activation: Real-time activation to Adobe and non-Adobe applications across all channels

AI Assistant: Generative AI integrated into platform for enhanced productivity (2025)

Use Case Playbooks: Pre-built playbooks for common use cases with asset generation (GA 2025)

Computed Attributes: Self-serve creation of profile attributes from event data

Federated Audience Composition: June 2025 feature federating datasets directly from warehouse

Collaboration: Real-Time CDP Collaboration for joint audience analysis with partners

Edition Options

B2C Edition: Purpose-built for B2C marketers unifying consumer data across systems

B2B Edition: Purpose-built for B2B marketers with lead and account profiles

B2P Edition: Unified B2C and B2B targeting same person across business lines

Prime vs Ultimate: Tiered packages with varying feature access and scale

Pricing Model

Licensing: Enterprise subscription as part of Adobe Experience Cloud

Packaging: Prime and Ultimate packages with different feature sets

Cost Consideration: Higher investment than standalone CDPs but includes platform ecosystem

Implementation: Complex implementation typically requiring Adobe partnership or system integrator

Best Use Cases

Adobe Real-Time CDP is ideal for enterprises already invested in Adobe Experience Cloud seeking unified customer data across the Adobe ecosystem. The platform excels at complex B2B and B2C organizations requiring multiple edition flexibility, companies prioritizing enterprise governance and compliance controls, and marketing teams wanting AI-powered insights through Adobe Sensei. Particularly strong for organizations with existing Adobe Target, Journey Optimizer, and Analytics investments.

CDP MARKET CONTEXT AND SELECTION GUIDANCE 2025
----------------------------------------

Market Landscape

The CDP market includes full-stack offerings like Adobe and Salesforce, warehouse-native tools like Hightouch, and composable options like Tealium and Treasure Data. Key 2025 trends include 57% growth in predictive traits adoption, growing dominance of CDP-warehouse integrations, and businesses rethinking martech stacks for competitiveness. Legacy CDPs continue struggling with batch-based pipelines limiting real-time personalization.

Platform Selection by Team Type

For engineering-led teams, Segment offers developer-friendly APIs with rapid POC deployment in days. For marketing-led teams, Tealium provides no-code audience building with 1,300+ integrations. For product teams with mobile apps, mParticle delivers best-in-class mobile SDKs and warehouse streaming. For Adobe ecosystem customers, Adobe Real-Time CDP provides native integration with Experience Cloud.

Platform Selection by Company Size

For startups and SMB, Segment Connections provides accessible entry with self-service pricing. For mid-market, Tealium and mParticle offer appropriate scale with enterprise features. For enterprise, all four platforms serve enterprise needs with Adobe providing the most comprehensive suite integration. Note that Tealium and Adobe may be cost-prohibitive for smaller organizations.

Implementation Considerations

Segment proof-of-concepts complete in days with developer resources. Tealium implementations take weeks to months depending on tag management complexity. mParticle implementation time is moderate with strong mobile SDK support. Adobe implementations are complex, typically requiring system integrator partnership and longer timelines. All vendors require contacting sales for enterprise pricing quotes.

Integration Comparison

Segment offers 550+ destinations with developer-friendly APIs. Tealium provides 1,300+ integrations through marketplace with tag management heritage. mParticle has 300+ API-based integrations with mobile strength. Adobe provides extensive Adobe ecosystem integration plus partner network. Integration breadth should be evaluated against specific destination requirements.
