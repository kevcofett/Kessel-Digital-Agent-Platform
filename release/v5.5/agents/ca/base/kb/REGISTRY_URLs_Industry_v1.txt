URL Index - Industry Verticals V7.0

Version 7.0 - Last Updated: December 2025

This index provides a comprehensive collection of URLs for industry-specific news sources, trade publications, and vertical-focused resources. Each entry documents primary resources for accessing news, research, and insights relevant to key industry verticals served by the Consulting Agent including Financial Services, Retail, CPG, QSR, and Travel and Hospitality.

Document Purpose

This reference serves as a central index for accessing industry-specific resources. When researching vertical trends, industry benchmarks, or sector-specific best practices, consult this index to locate authoritative sources and current information. URLs are organized by industry vertical to facilitate rapid resource discovery.

Financial Services and Fintech

Publications covering financial services, banking, insurance, wealth management, payments, and fintech include FinTech Magazine for fintech news and research, Finextra for financial technology news with daily updates, The Fintech Times for weekly fintech coverage, and FFNews for daily financial services news. BCG Fintech provides strategic insights and research on financial services. American Banker offers daily banking industry news and research. Banking Dive provides weekly banking news and resources. PaymentsSource covers weekly payments industry news and resources.

Key topics within financial services include digital banking transformation, open banking and APIs, embedded finance, payments innovation, regulatory technology, wealth management technology, insurance technology, and cryptocurrency and blockchain applications in finance. These publications provide ongoing coverage of trends, vendor announcements, and regulatory developments affecting financial services marketing and customer experience.

Retail and Ecommerce

Retail Dive provides daily retail news, research, and trends at retail-focused coverage including news, research, and trend analysis. Modern Retail offers daily coverage of retail news and research with focus on direct-to-consumer brands and digital commerce. NRF through the National Retail Federation provides weekly news, research, resources, and industry data supporting retail advocacy and education. Retail TouchPoints delivers daily retail news, research, and resources with strong loyalty program coverage.

Additional retail publications include Practical Ecommerce for weekly ecommerce news and resources, Digital Commerce 360 for daily ecommerce news, research, and data including top retailer rankings, Chain Store Age for weekly retail news and resources focused on chain retailers, and Retail Week for weekly UK and global retail news and research.

Key topics within retail and ecommerce include omnichannel commerce, retail media networks, customer loyalty programs, personalization and recommendations, inventory and supply chain, store technology and point of sale, ecommerce platforms and marketplaces, and payment and checkout optimization. These publications cover both traditional retail and digital commerce with increasing focus on the convergence of online and offline channels.

Consumer Packaged Goods CPG

Consumer Goods Technology provides weekly CPG news, research, and resources focused on technology adoption in the consumer goods industry. Food Dive delivers daily food and beverage news, research, and trends covering the food manufacturing and distribution sectors. CPG Matters offers weekly CPG news and resources. YouGov provides weekly research, data, and consumer insights resources with strong CPG brand tracking capabilities. Grocery Dive covers daily grocery industry news and trends.

Key topics within CPG include direct-to-consumer strategies, retail media partnerships, first-party data collection, supply chain visibility, sustainability and ESG, product innovation pipelines, consumer insights and research, and trade promotion optimization. CPG publications increasingly cover the intersection of brand marketing, retail partnerships, and data-driven decision making.

Quick Service Restaurants QSR

QSR Magazine provides weekly QSR news, research, resources, and rankings including the QSR 50 annual ranking of top quick service brands. Restaurant Business delivers daily restaurant industry news, research, and data covering both QSR and full service segments. Nations Restaurant News offers daily news, research, and rankings including the Top 500 restaurant chains. Fast Casual covers weekly fast casual segment news and resources. Restaurant Dive provides daily restaurant news and trends.

Key topics within QSR include digital ordering and delivery, loyalty program innovation, menu personalization, labor and workforce management, kitchen technology and automation, drive-through optimization, ghost kitchens and virtual brands, and franchisee relationships. QSR publications focus heavily on technology adoption, customer experience innovation, and operational efficiency in the restaurant industry.

Travel and Hospitality

Skift provides daily travel news, research, data, and trends as the leading travel industry publication covering airlines, hotels, online travel, and destinations. Travel Weekly delivers weekly travel industry news and research. Hotel Dive offers daily hotel industry news and trends. McKinsey Travel provides monthly strategic insights and research on the travel sector. Hospitality Net covers daily hospitality news and resources.

Additional travel publications include PKF Hospitality for monthly research and data focused on hotel performance metrics and benchmarking, EHL Hospitality Insights for weekly hospitality insights and research from Ecole hoteliere de Lausanne, Hotel News Resource for daily hotel industry news, and Phocuswire for daily travel technology news and research focusing on the intersection of travel and technology.

Key topics within travel and hospitality include revenue management and dynamic pricing, guest experience personalization, loyalty program partnerships, distribution channel strategy, sustainability and responsible travel, business travel recovery, alternative accommodations, and destination marketing. Travel publications cover the full ecosystem from airlines and hotels to online travel agencies and metasearch platforms.

Industry Events and Conferences

Marketing and advertising conferences include Adobe Summit, Cannes Lions, Advertising Week Europe, Advertising Week NYC, MarTech Conference, INBOUND by HubSpot, DMEXCO, Content Marketing World, MarketingProfs B2B Forum, and ANA Masters of Marketing.

AdTech and programmatic conferences include Programmatic I/O Las Vegas, Programmatic I/O NYC, MAD Fest London, Programmatic Pioneers Summit, ATS London, and POSSIBLE by MMA.

Retail and ecommerce conferences include NRF Retails Big Show, Shoptalk Spring, Shoptalk Fall, eTail West, eTail East, and Groceryshop.

Financial services conferences include Money20/20 USA, Money20/20 Europe, Money20/20 Asia, FinovateFall, FinovateSpring, FinovateEurope, MoneyLIVE Summit, Fintech Meetup, and Singapore Fintech Festival.

Loyalty and customer experience conferences include Loyalty360 Loyalty Expo, Loyalty360 CX Fest, Forrester CX Summit NA, Customer Experience Exchange, and CRMC.

Restaurant conferences include MURTEC, QSR Evolution Conference, FSTEC, and the National Restaurant Association Show.

Travel conferences include HITEC, The Phocuswright Conference, World Travel Market, and ITB Berlin.
