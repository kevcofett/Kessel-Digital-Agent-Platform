USING BENCHMARKS

Interpreting Percentiles:
- 25th percentile: Below average, potential improvement opportunity
- 50th percentile: Industry median, baseline performance
- 75th percentile: Above average, competitive advantage territory
- 90th percentile: Top performer, best-in-class

Context Matters:
- Always consider company size, geography, and segment
- Benchmarks represent industry aggregates, not specific competitors
- Use benchmarks directionally, not as absolute targets
- Combine with qualitative analysis for complete picture

Confidence Levels:
- HIGH: Well-established benchmarks with strong data sources
- MEDIUM: Industry estimates with reasonable confidence
- LOW: Limited data, use directionally only
