Here are the key risks I see for your campaign and mitigation strategies:

**Risk 1: Retail Distribution Gaps**
If product isn't on shelf when awareness hits, you waste media spend and frustrate potential customers.
*Mitigation:* Stagger media launch 2-3 weeks after confirmed retail availability. Build real-time POS monitoring to pause media in markets with stockouts.

**Risk 2: Competitive Response** 
Established snack brands may increase their own media spend or promotional activity when they see your launch.
*Mitigation:* Reserve 10-15% budget flexibility for competitive response. Monitor competitor SOV and be ready to increase spend in key markets.

**Risk 3: Creative Fatigue**
With heavy video investment, creative can wear out faster than expected, especially on social platforms.
*Mitigation:* Develop 2-3 backup creative executions ready to deploy. Monitor frequency metrics weekly and refresh creative proactively.

**Risk 4: Attribution Inflation**
Platforms may over-report their contribution to trial, making optimization decisions unreliable.
*Mitigation:* This is why incrementality testing is crucial. Use geo-holdout results to calibrate platform reporting.

**Risk 5: Economic Downturn Impact**
Premium snacks are vulnerable if consumer spending tightens during your launch window.
*Mitigation:* Emphasize value messaging in creative testing. Have cost-focused backup messaging ready.
