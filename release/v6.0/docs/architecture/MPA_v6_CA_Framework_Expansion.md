## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Consulting Agent (CA) serves as an AI-powered strategic consulting assistant focused on:
- **Deep Research & Discovery** - Accelerating situation analysis and fact-finding
- **Assessment & Evaluation** - Systematic analysis of challenges and opportunities
- **Business Case Development** - Quantified, fully-qualified investment recommendations
- **Roadmap & Prioritization** - Sequenced implementation plans with dependencies

This document expands the original 32 consulting frameworks to **60+ frameworks** and adds **35+ analytical models** specifically designed for strategic consulting use cases.
