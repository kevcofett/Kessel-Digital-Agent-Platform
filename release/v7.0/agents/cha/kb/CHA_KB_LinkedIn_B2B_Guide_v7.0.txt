SECTION 1 - LINKEDIN CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE
================================================================================

CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVES

AWARENESS OBJECTIVES

BRAND AWARENESS
- Maximize impressions to target audience
- Charged by impressions (CPM)
- Best for: Brand building, thought leadership
- Typical CPM: $8-15

CONSIDERATION OBJECTIVES

WEBSITE VISITS
- Drive traffic to website
- Optimize for landing page views
- Charged by click (CPC) or impressions
- Typical CPC: $5-12

ENGAGEMENT
- Increase post interactions
- Reactions, comments, shares, follows
- Charged by engagement or impressions
- Best for: Content amplification

VIDEO VIEWS
- Maximize video consumption
- Views counted at 2+ seconds
- Charged by view or impressions
- Best for: Video content distribution

CONVERSION OBJECTIVES

LEAD GENERATION
- Collect leads via native Lead Gen Forms
- Pre-filled with LinkedIn profile data
- Charged by click or impressions
- Best for: B2B lead capture

WEBSITE CONVERSIONS
- Drive specific website actions
- Requires Insight Tag implementation
- Charged by click or impressions
- Best for: Demo requests, downloads

JOB APPLICANTS
- Drive applications for open roles
- Talent acquisition focus
- Charged by click or impressions

CAMPAIGN TYPES

SPONSORED CONTENT
- Native feed placements
- Single image, carousel, video, document
- Highest engagement format
- Appears in member feeds

SPONSORED MESSAGING

MESSAGE ADS
- Direct inbox messages
- Personal, conversational format
- Higher cost, higher engagement
- Strict frequency limits apply

CONVERSATION ADS
- Interactive message experiences
- Multiple CTA paths
- Decision tree format
- Good for: Qualification, routing

LEAD GEN FORMS
- Native form attached to ads
- Pre-filled from profile
- High completion rates
- Reduces friction vs landing pages

TEXT AND DYNAMIC ADS

TEXT ADS
- Right rail and top banner
- Lowest cost format
- Lower engagement
- Good for: Always-on presence

SPOTLIGHT ADS
- Personalized with member photo/name
- Desktop right rail
- Higher engagement than text
- Good for: Personalized messaging

FOLLOWER ADS
- Drive company page follows
- Personalized format
- Good for: Audience building

SECTION 2 - AUDIENCE TARGETING
================================================================================

PROFESSIONAL TARGETING

COMPANY TARGETING

COMPANY NAME
- Target specific companies by name
- Account-based marketing essential
- Upload account lists
- Minimum: 300 members for targeting

COMPANY SIZE
- Employee count ranges
- 1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 201-500, 501-1000, 1001-5000, 5001-10000, 10001+
- Self-reported by companies

COMPANY INDUSTRY
- LinkedIn industry taxonomy
- 148 industries available
- Can combine multiple
- Includes sub-industries

COMPANY REVENUE
- Estimated annual revenue
- Ranges from under 1M to 10B+
- Based on LinkedIn data
- Not available for all companies

COMPANY GROWTH RATE
- Headcount growth percentage
- Identifies fast-growing companies
- Good for: Technology, startups

JOB TARGETING

JOB TITLE
- Specific title targeting
- Standardized by LinkedIn
- Most precise professional targeting
- Can be narrow, consider synonyms

JOB FUNCTION
- Broader than title
- 26 functions available
- Accounting, Engineering, Marketing, Sales, etc.
- Good for: Broader reach

JOB SENIORITY
- Unpaid, Entry, Senior, Manager, Director, VP, CXO, Owner/Partner
- Key B2B targeting lever
- Combine with function for precision

YEARS OF EXPERIENCE
- Professional experience ranges
- 1-2, 3-5, 6-10, 11+ years
- Good for: Career stage targeting

SKILLS AND INTERESTS

MEMBER SKILLS
- Skills listed on profiles
- 35,000+ skills available
- Self-reported
- Good for: Technical targeting

MEMBER INTERESTS
- Inferred from behavior
- Content engagement signals
- Broader than explicit data

MEMBER GROUPS
- Target group members
- Industry and professional groups
- High intent signal
- Limited scale

EDUCATION TARGETING

SCHOOLS
- Specific university targeting
- Alumni targeting
- Good for: Recruiting, education

DEGREES
- Degree type (BA, MBA, PhD)
- Good for: Education requirements

FIELDS OF STUDY
- Academic discipline
- Computer Science, Business, etc.
- Good for: Technical roles

MATCHED AUDIENCES

WEBSITE RETARGETING
- Insight Tag required
- Segment by pages visited
- 90-day lookback maximum
- Minimum: 300 members

CONTACT TARGETING
- Upload email lists
- Match rate: 30-50% typical
- Refresh monthly
- Good for: ABM, CRM sync

COMPANY TARGETING
- Upload company lists
- Target employees of companies
- Good for: Account-based marketing
- Combine with title/seniority

LOOKALIKE AUDIENCES
- Based on matched audiences
- Expand reach to similar members
- Lower precision than source
- Good for: Prospecting scale

SECTION 3 - ACCOUNT-BASED MARKETING
================================================================================

ABM STRATEGY ON LINKEDIN

ABM DEFINITION
Concentrate marketing resources on specific high-value target accounts rather than broad market segments.

LINKEDIN ABM ADVANTAGES
- Precise company targeting
- Decision-maker identification
- Multi-stakeholder reach
- Engagement tracking by account

ABM TIERS

TIER 1 ACCOUNTS (1:1)
- Highest value, named accounts
- Fully customized messaging
- Dedicated campaigns per account
- High touch, low volume
- Typical: 10-50 accounts

TIER 2 ACCOUNTS (1:FEW)
- High value clusters
- Grouped by industry or need
- Semi-customized messaging
- Medium touch
- Typical: 50-200 accounts

TIER 3 ACCOUNTS (1:MANY)
- Broader target account list
- Standardized messaging
- Scalable approach
- Lower touch
- Typical: 200-1000 accounts

ABM CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE

ACCOUNT LIST CREATION
- Define ideal customer profile
- Identify target companies
- Upload to LinkedIn Campaign Manager
- Add job function and seniority layers

BUYING COMMITTEE TARGETING
- Map stakeholders by role
- Target decision makers (Director+)
- Target influencers (Managers)
- Target end users (Individual contributors)

MESSAGE SEQUENCING
- Awareness: Thought leadership, industry trends
- Education: Solution overview, use cases
- Consideration: Case studies, comparisons
- Decision: Demos, consultations, offers

ABM MEASUREMENT

ACCOUNT ENGAGEMENT METRICS
- Accounts reached
- Accounts engaged
- Engagement by account
- Content interactions per account

ENGAGEMENT SCORING
Weight interactions by value:
- Ad impression: 1 point
- Click: 5 points
- Video view: 3 points
- Lead form open: 10 points
- Lead form submit: 25 points

ACCOUNT PROGRESSION
Track accounts through stages:
- Unaware: No engagement
- Aware: Impressions only
- Engaged: Clicks or interactions
- MQL: Lead form submission
- Opportunity: Sales accepted

SECTION 4 - LEAD GENERATION
================================================================================

LEAD GEN FORMS

FORM ADVANTAGES
- Pre-filled from LinkedIn profile
- No landing page needed
- Mobile optimized
- Higher completion rates

FORM FIELDS

PROFILE FIELDS (PRE-FILLED)
- First name, last name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Company name
- Job title
- Company size
- Industry

CUSTOM QUESTIONS
- Single line text
- Single select dropdown
- Checkbox (yes/no)
- Maximum 3 custom questions recommended

FORM BEST PRACTICES
- Minimize fields (5-7 total)
- Use pre-filled where possible
- Custom questions for qualification only
- Clear privacy policy link

LEAD QUALITY OPTIMIZATION

QUALIFICATION FIELDS
Add fields to improve quality:
- Budget authority
- Timeline
- Specific needs
- Company size verification

QUALITY VS VOLUME TRADE-OFF
- More fields = fewer leads, higher quality
- Fewer fields = more leads, lower quality
- Test to find optimal balance

LEAD ROUTING

CRM INTEGRATION
- Sync leads directly to CRM
- Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, etc.
- Real-time or batch sync
- Map fields correctly

LEAD SCORING
Score leads based on:
- Job seniority (weight: high)
- Company size (weight: medium)
- Form answers (weight: varies)
- Engagement history (weight: low)

FOLLOW-UP TIMING
- Respond within 5 minutes ideal
- Within 1 hour acceptable
- Beyond 24 hours: Significant drop-off
- Automate initial response

LEAD GEN BENCHMARKS

FORM COMPLETION RATE
- Good: 10-15%
- Excellent: 15-25%
- Below 10%: Simplify form

COST PER LEAD
- Industry average: $50-150
- Enterprise B2B: $100-300
- SMB focused: $30-75

LEAD TO MQL RATE
- Good: 20-40%
- Depends on qualification criteria
- Track and optimize over time

SECTION 5 - BIDDING AND BUDGETING
================================================================================

BIDDING OPTIONS

MAXIMUM DELIVERY
- LinkedIn optimizes for results
- Spends full daily budget
- Best for: Learning, volume focus
- May sacrifice efficiency

TARGET COST
- Set target cost per result
- Algorithm optimizes to target
- May limit delivery if too aggressive
- Best for: Efficiency focus

MANUAL BIDDING
- Set maximum bid per click/impression
- Full control
- Requires active management
- Best for: Experienced advertisers

BID GUIDANCE

SUGGESTED BID RANGE
LinkedIn provides range based on:
- Audience competitiveness
- Historical performance
- Current demand

BID RECOMMENDATIONS
- Start at suggested bid midpoint
- Increase if underdelivering
- Decrease if overspending
- Test different levels

AUCTION DYNAMICS
- Second-price auction
- Winner pays minimum to win
- Relevance score impacts delivery
- Quality affects actual cost

BUDGET STRUCTURE

MINIMUM BUDGETS
- Daily minimum: $10/day
- Recommended minimum: $50/day
- Meaningful test: $100+/day

BUDGET BY CAMPAIGN TYPE
- Sponsored Content: $100-500/day
- Message Ads: $50-200/day
- Text Ads: $25-100/day

BUDGET ALLOCATION BY FUNNEL
- Awareness: 20-30%
- Consideration: 30-40%
- Conversion: 30-50%

COST BENCHMARKS

SPONSORED CONTENT
- CPC: $5-12 average
- CPM: $30-80 average
- Cost per lead: $50-150

MESSAGE ADS
- Cost per send: $0.50-1.00
- Open rate: 30-50%
- CTR: 3-8%

TEXT ADS
- CPC: $3-8 average
- CPM: $15-40 average
- Lower engagement

SECTION 6 - CREATIVE BEST PRACTICES
================================================================================

SPONSORED CONTENT CREATIVE

SINGLE IMAGE
- Recommended size: 1200x627 pixels
- File size: Under 5MB
- Aspect ratio: 1.91:1
- Include clear CTA

CAROUSEL
- 2-10 cards
- Tell sequential story
- Each card needs strong visual
- Use for: Product features, process steps

VIDEO
- Length: 15-90 seconds optimal
- Captions required (sound off default)
- Vertical (9:16) and horizontal (16:9)
- First 10 seconds critical

DOCUMENT ADS
- PDF format
- Gated or ungated
- Thought leadership content
- Good for: White papers, guides

COPY BEST PRACTICES

HEADLINE
- 150 characters maximum
- Lead with value proposition
- Include specific benefit
- Test question vs statement

INTRODUCTORY TEXT
- 600 characters maximum
- Hook in first line
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include clear CTA

MESSAGING FOR B2B

EFFECTIVE ANGLES
- Business outcomes focus
- Peer validation (social proof)
- Industry-specific relevance
- Competitive differentiation

AVOID
- Consumer-style messaging
- Feature-only focus
- Generic value propositions
- Overly promotional tone

MESSAGE ADS CREATIVE

SUBJECT LINE
- 60 characters maximum
- Personalization tokens available
- A/B test aggressively
- Avoid spam triggers

MESSAGE BODY
- 1,500 characters maximum
- Conversational tone
- Single clear CTA
- Personalization recommended

SENDER PROFILE
- Use real person, not company
- Senior title builds credibility
- Profile photo matters
- Complete LinkedIn profile

SECTION 7 - MEASUREMENT AND OPTIMIZATION
================================================================================

INSIGHT TAG

PURPOSE
- Track website conversions
- Build retargeting audiences
- Enable conversion tracking
- Demographic insights

IMPLEMENTATION
- Place on all website pages
- Event-specific tracking for conversions
- Test with LinkedIn tag helper
- Verify data flowing

CONVERSION ACTIONS

STANDARD CONVERSIONS
- URL-based (thank you page)
- Event-based (button click)
- Lead Gen Form submission

CONVERSION WINDOW
- Click-through: 30 days
- View-through: 7 days
- Post-click attribution standard

REPORTING METRICS

DELIVERY METRICS
- Impressions
- Reach (unique members)
- Frequency
- Spend

ENGAGEMENT METRICS
- Clicks
- CTR (click-through rate)
- Social actions (likes, comments, shares)
- Video views and completion rate

CONVERSION METRICS
- Conversions
- Conversion rate
- Cost per conversion
- Conversion value

DEMOGRAPHICS REPORTING
- Job function breakdown
- Seniority breakdown
- Industry breakdown
- Company size breakdown
- Company breakdown (if targeting)

OPTIMIZATION APPROACH

WEEKLY OPTIMIZATION
- Review performance by audience segment
- Pause underperforming audiences
- Increase budget on top performers
- Refresh creative if CTR declining

AUDIENCE OPTIMIZATION
- Exclude converted members
- Add negative attributes
- Test lookalike expansion
- Refine company lists

CREATIVE OPTIMIZATION
- Test headline variations
- Test image vs video
- Rotate creative every 2-4 weeks
- Monitor relevance scores

SECTION 8 - AGENT APPLICATION GUIDANCE
================================================================================

WHEN TO RECOMMEND LINKEDIN

IDEAL USE CASES
- B2B lead generation
- Account-based marketing
- Professional services
- Technology products
- Recruiting and employer brand

LESS IDEAL USE CASES
- B2C products
- Low-cost impulse purchases
- Mass market reach
- Brand awareness at scale

BUDGET RECOMMENDATIONS

MINIMUM VIABLE TEST
- $5,000/month minimum
- 60-90 day test period
- Focus on single objective
- Single audience to start

SCALING BUDGETS
- $10,000+/month for optimization
- $25,000+/month for full-funnel
- $50,000+/month for enterprise ABM

CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE RECOMMENDATIONS

STARTER STRUCTURE
- Sponsored Content (awareness/consideration)
- Lead Gen Forms (conversion)
- Retargeting (engaged prospects)

ADVANCED STRUCTURE
- Thought leadership (awareness)
- Case studies (consideration)
- Demo offers (conversion)
- ABM layer (key accounts)
- Message Ads (high-value prospects)

WARNING SIGNS

PERFORMANCE ISSUES
- CTR below 0.3%: Creative or targeting issue
- CPL above 2x target: Refine audience
- Low form completion: Simplify form
- Frequency above 4: Expand audience

COMMON MISTAKES
- Targeting too narrow
- Too many form fields
- Ignoring retargeting
- Not excluding converters
- Same creative too long

LINKEDIN-SPECIFIC ROAS NOTE
- LinkedIn does not use standard ROAS calculation
- Track cost per lead vs lead value manually
- Calculate true ROAS: (Pipeline Value - Ad Spend) / Ad Spend
