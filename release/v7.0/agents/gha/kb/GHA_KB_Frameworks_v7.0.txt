01 GROWTH HACKING FRAMEWORKS CORE MODELS
==================================================

FRAMEWORK AARRR DAVE MCCLURE PIRATE METRICS
Description: The original funnel used to analyze and design growth systems.
Stages:
- Acquisition: Get users in the door
- Activation: Deliver first wow moment
- Retention: Keep them coming back
- Referral: Turn them into advocates
- Revenue: Monetize usage

Use Case: Dropbox
Tactic: Offered 250MB storage for each referral. Activation and referral were stacked.
Result: 3900 percent growth in 15 months.

---

FRAMEWORK AAARRRP MODERN LIFECYCLE EXPANSION
Adds:
- Reactivation: Revive inactive users
- Productization: Bake growth into the product through product-led growth
- Personalization: Use behavioral data for lifecycle nudges

Use Case: Calm App
Tactic: Sent personalized meditation stats and leaderboards to re-engage users.
Layer: Reactivation plus Personalization

---

FRAMEWORK GROWTH LOOP MODEL
Concept: Funnels are linear but loops are compounding.
Structure: Inputs to Triggers to Action to Output which refeeds system

Examples:
- Content Loop: User posts then Friends see then They join then They post
- Product Loop: Invite friend to unlock premium then friend invites others
- Transaction Loop: User buys then gets rewards then reinvests then buys again

Use Case: Notion
Loop: Share template then other user signs up then creates then shares again.

---

FRAMEWORK HOOK MODEL BY NIR EYAL
Cycle:
- Trigger which can be external or internal
- Action
- Variable Reward
- Investment

Use Case: Instagram
Trigger: Notification
Action: Tap app
Reward: New likes and comments
Investment: Post photo equals data fuel for future notifications

---

FRAMEWORK POWER USER CURVE
Focus: Time-based usage measuring if 10 percent of users come back more than X times in Y days.
Use Case: Slack
Goal: 2000 messages across org indicates a sticky metric
Tactic: Product usage nudges tied to team onboarding plus integrations

---

FRAMEWORK NORTH STAR METRIC MODEL
Find the one metric that best correlates with long-term success.

Examples:
- Airbnb: Nights booked
- Facebook: Monthly active users
- Spotify: Minutes streamed
- Duolingo: Number of lessons completed

---

FRAMEWORK HIGH-TEMPO TESTING BY SEAN ELLIS
Cycle:
- Prioritize 3 to 5 weekly experiments
- Use ICE or PIE scoring to stack them
- Deploy then learn then keep or kill fast

Use Case: GrowthHackers.com
Over 30 weekly growth tests led to identifying one referral loop that scaled platform growth.

02 PSYCHOLOGICAL PRINCIPLES THAT POWER GROWTH
==================================================

SCARCITY
Tactic: Limited-time or limited-access features
Case: Clubhouse invite-only created intense viral fear of missing out

SOCIAL PROOF
Tactic: Join 94000 other designers messaging
Case: Figma launched via community events plus social leaderboards

MIMETIC DESIRE FROM RENE GIRARD
Tactic: We want what others want
Case: BeReal exclusivity triggered curiosity through friend feeds

CURIOSITY LOOPS
Tactic: Unlock hidden feature or delayed reward
Case: Duolingo daily streak chests

REWARD PREDICTION ERROR
Tactic: User expects X but gets Y which triggers dopamine
Case: Tinder Super Likes plus unexpected matches

IDENTITY TRIGGERS
Tactic: Let user define themselves via behavior
Case: Spotify Wrapped which people share because it reflects them

EFFORT JUSTIFICATION
Tactic: If they worked for it they value it more
Case: Reddit Karma where you grind then earn then brag

LOSS AVERSION
Tactic: You will lose this reward if you do not act
Case: Amazon Lightning Deals with countdown timers

SCARCITY COMBINED WITH STATUS
Tactic: Not everyone gets in and not everyone should
Case: American Express Black Card with invite-only and myth-building

03 EXECUTION MODELS BY LIFECYCLE STAGE
==================================================

ACQUISITION
Tactics:
- Waitlists with reward stacking like Morning Brew
- Viral referral ladders like Robinhood
- Challenger campaigns with try us instead messaging
- Side project SEO traps like free calculator to product

ACTIVATION
Tactics:
- Gamified onboarding with progress bar and steps remaining
- Product tours with soft personalization
- Surprise and delight with hidden feature after first use
- Use-case triggered nudges like Calendly auto-sending value props after first meeting

USAGE EXPANSION
Tactics:
- Secret multiplier zones like mystery cashback
- Weekly usage streaks
- Spend across 3 categories to get bonus
- Product suggestions based on unusual behavior

RETENTION
Tactics:
- Dynamic email triggers based on inactivity patterns
- Personalized push nudges with past behavior callouts
- Limited edition loyalty missions
- Return now and reclaim your reward messaging

RE-ENGAGEMENT
Tactics:
- Clever reactivation campaigns like Are we on a break
- Time-boxed challenges
- Reward retrieval with Your bonus is expiring in 2 hours

LOYALTY
Tactics:
- Gamified tier systems with prestige plus perks
- Shareable reward summaries like Top 2 percent cashback earners
- Family and team plans to create network lock-in
- UGC incentives for long-term users
