GROWTH HACKING FRAMEWORKS
==================================================

AARRR FUNNEL
Description: A classic framework that breaks growth into five key stages: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, and Revenue.
Use Cases: Startup growth modeling, Funnel diagnostics, Retention prioritization
Complexity Level: Medium
Relevance Score: 10

GROWTH LOOPS
Description: A self-reinforcing system where each user action contributes to acquiring more users, creating compounding effects.
Use Cases: Product-led growth, Marketplace virality, User-generated content
Complexity Level: High
Relevance Score: 9

NORTH STAR METRIC
Description: A singular, guiding metric that best reflects the core value delivered to customers and long-term business growth.
Use Cases: Executive alignment, Product prioritization, Growth team OKRs
Complexity Level: Medium
Relevance Score: 9

ICE PRIORITIZATION
Description: A simple scoring model that evaluates ideas based on Impact, Confidence, and Effort.
Use Cases: Growth backlog planning, Experiment pipeline management
Complexity Level: Low
Relevance Score: 8
