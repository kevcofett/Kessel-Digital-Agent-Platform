SECTION 1 - FULL MEDIA PLAN TEMPLATE
================================================================================

DOCUMENT STRUCTURE

PAGE 1 - COVER PAGE
- Document title: [Campaign Name] Media Plan
- Client name
- Prepared by and date
- Version number
- Confidentiality notice

PAGE 2 - TABLE OF CONTENTS
- Auto-generated section list
- Page numbers for navigation

PAGES 3-4 - EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY STRUCTURE
Paragraph 1 - Overview
"This media plan recommends a $[Total Budget] investment across [Number] channels over [Duration] to achieve [Primary Objective] for [Client Name]."

Paragraph 2 - Strategy Highlights
"The strategy prioritizes [Key Decision 1] and [Key Decision 2] based on [Primary Rationale]. [Secondary Supporting Point]."

Paragraph 3 - Expected Outcomes
"We project [Primary Metric] of [Range] with [Confidence Level] confidence. Secondary outcomes include [Metric 2] and [Metric 3]."

Paragraph 4 - Investment Summary
"Budget allocation emphasizes [Top Channel] at [Percent] of spend, followed by [Second Channel] at [Percent]. This mix optimizes for [Optimization Goal]."

Paragraph 5 - Key Risks and Mitigations
"Primary risks include [Risk 1] and [Risk 2]. Mitigation strategies include [Strategy 1] and [Strategy 2]."

Investment Summary Table
- Column headers: Channel, Budget, Percent of Total, Primary Role
- One row per channel
- Total row at bottom

PAGES 5-6 - OBJECTIVES AND KPIS

OBJECTIVES SECTION STRUCTURE
Business Objective Statement
"[Client Name] seeks to [Business Goal] by [Timeframe], targeting [Quantified Outcome]."

Marketing Objective Statement
"This campaign will [Marketing Goal] among [Target Audience], measured by [Primary KPI]."

KPI Table
- Column headers: KPI, Target, Measurement Method, Reporting Frequency
- Primary KPI row
- Secondary KPI rows (2-3)
- Guardrail metric rows (1-2)

Success Criteria Definition
"Campaign success is defined as achieving [Primary Target] while maintaining [Guardrail Metric] within [Acceptable Range]."

PAGES 7-8 - TARGET AUDIENCE

AUDIENCE SECTION STRUCTURE
Primary Audience Profile
- Demographics: Age, gender, income, geography
- Psychographics: Interests, values, lifestyle
- Behaviors: Media consumption, purchase patterns
- Size estimate: Addressable population

Audience Prioritization Table
- Column headers: Segment, Priority, Size, Value Index, Primary Channel
- Rows for each segment (3-5 segments)

Targeting Approach
"Targeting strategy employs [Approach Type] using [Data Sources]. Primary signals include [Signal 1], [Signal 2], and [Signal 3]."

PAGES 9-12 - CHANNEL STRATEGY

CHANNEL STRATEGY OVERVIEW
Strategy Narrative
"The channel mix balances [Goal 1] and [Goal 2] through a combination of [Channel Categories]. [Rationale for Mix]."

Channel Role Framework Table
- Column headers: Channel, Role, Objective Contribution, Budget Share
- Rows for each channel

Channel Mix Rationale
For each channel, one paragraph covering:
- Why selected
- Role in the mix
- Expected contribution
- Key tactics

PAGES 13-18 - CHANNEL DETAILS

CHANNEL DETAIL PAGE TEMPLATE (One per channel)

Channel Header
- Channel name
- Budget: $[Amount] ([Percent] of total)
- Flight dates: [Start] to [End]

Channel Objectives
- Primary objective for this channel
- Contribution to overall goals

Tactics and Placements
- Placement types selected
- Targeting parameters
- Creative formats

Performance Projections Table
- Column headers: Metric, Projected Value, Benchmark, Variance
- Rows: Impressions, Reach, Frequency, CTR, Conversions, CPA

Budget Pacing
- Monthly or weekly allocation
- Flighting rationale

PAGES 19-20 - MEASUREMENT FRAMEWORK

MEASUREMENT SECTION STRUCTURE
Measurement Approach Overview
"Campaign measurement employs [Attribution Model] with [Primary Tool]. Reporting cadence is [Frequency] with [Dashboard/Report Type]."

Metrics Hierarchy Table
- Column headers: Level, Metric, Target, Source, Owner
- Rows organized by: Business Metrics, Marketing Metrics, Channel Metrics

Attribution Methodology
- Model selected and rationale
- Limitations acknowledged
- Validation approach

Reporting Schedule
- Weekly reports: Contents and recipients
- Monthly reports: Contents and recipients
- Final report: Timeline and contents

PAGES 21-22 - TIMELINE AND FLIGHTING

TIMELINE SECTION STRUCTURE
Campaign Calendar Visual
- Gantt-style chart showing:
- Overall campaign duration
- Channel flight dates
- Key milestones
- Optimization checkpoints

Milestone Table
- Column headers: Milestone, Date, Owner, Dependencies
- Rows for each key date

Flighting Rationale
"Budget pacing follows [Pattern] to align with [Business Factor]. Heavy-up periods include [Period 1] due to [Reason]."

PAGES 23-24 - OPTIMIZATION APPROACH

OPTIMIZATION SECTION STRUCTURE
Optimization Philosophy
"In-flight optimization prioritizes [Primary Goal] while maintaining [Constraint]. Reallocation threshold is [Percent] with [Approval Process]."

Optimization Triggers Table
- Column headers: Trigger, Threshold, Action, Timeline
- Rows for efficiency, pacing, engagement triggers

Testing Agenda
- Planned tests during campaign
- Hypothesis for each
- Success criteria

PAGE 25+ - APPENDIX

APPENDIX CONTENTS
A - Benchmark Sources
- Table of benchmarks used with sources and dates

B - Assumptions Log
- All assumptions made during planning
- Impact if assumption is wrong

C - Glossary
- Definitions of key terms

D - Detailed Budget Tables
- Line-item budget breakdown
