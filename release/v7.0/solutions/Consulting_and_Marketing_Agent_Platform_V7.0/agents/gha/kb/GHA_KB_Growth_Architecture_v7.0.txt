SECTION 7 - KEY METRICS AND KPIS
================================================================================

CAC (CUSTOMER ACQUISITION COST)
Complexity: Low | Relevance: 10/10

Total cost spent on acquiring customers divided by number of customers acquired.

LTV (LIFETIME VALUE)
Complexity: Medium | Relevance: 10/10

Projected net revenue from a customer over their lifetime with the product.

ACTIVATION RATE
Complexity: Medium | Relevance: 9/10

Percentage of users who reach a predefined value milestone shortly after signup.

CHURN RATE
Complexity: Low | Relevance: 9/10

The percentage of users who stop using the product over a given period.

RETENTION CURVE
Complexity: Medium | Relevance: 8/10

A visualization of how many users continue engaging over time after signup.
