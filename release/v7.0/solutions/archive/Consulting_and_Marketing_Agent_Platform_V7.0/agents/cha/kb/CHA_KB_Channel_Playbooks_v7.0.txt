CHA CHANNEL PLAYBOOKS v1

PURPOSE

This document provides role-based channel selection guidance for the Channel Strategy Agent. Reference this document when matching channels to objectives, building channel mix strategies, and identifying synergy opportunities.

FUNNEL-BASED CHANNEL MAPPING

AWARENESS CHANNELS (Top Funnel)

Purpose: Build reach, introduce brand, generate demand
Primary Metrics: Reach, Frequency, CPM, Brand Lift

Recommended Channels:
- CTV_STREAMING: Full-screen premium video
- CTV_PREMIUM: Live sports and events
- YOUTUBE: Largest video platform
- OLV: Programmatic video scale
- PROG_DISPLAY_OPEN: Broad reach at low cost
- PROG_DISPLAY_PMP: Quality display inventory
- TIKTOK: Gen Z and Millennial reach
- AUDIO_STREAMING: High completion audio
- DOOH: Location-based visibility

Typical Budget Allocation: 20-40% of total spend
Minimum Flight: 4-6 weeks for measurable impact

CONSIDERATION CHANNELS (Mid Funnel)

Purpose: Educate prospects, drive engagement, nurture interest
Primary Metrics: CTR, Engagement Rate, Site Visits, VCR

Recommended Channels:
- META: Strong engagement targeting
- LINKEDIN: B2B consideration essential
- PINTEREST: High purchase intent
- REDDIT: Community engagement
- PODCAST: Host-read trust building
- PODCAST_HOST_READ: Premium consideration
- NATIVE: Content discovery
- OLV: Video consideration content

Typical Budget Allocation: 20-30% of total spend
Minimum Flight: 4-8 weeks for nurturing

CONVERSION CHANNELS (Bottom Funnel)

Purpose: Capture demand, drive action, maximize ROAS
Primary Metrics: CVR, CPA, ROAS, CAC

Recommended Channels:
- PAID_SEARCH: Highest intent capture
- SHOPPING: Product-specific conversion
- AMAZON_ADS: Purchase intent data
- RETAIL_MEDIA: Closed-loop attribution
- EMAIL: Owned channel conversion
- SMS: High engagement action
- PROG_DISPLAY_PMP: Retargeting
- META: Lower funnel optimization

Typical Budget Allocation: 30-50% of total spend
Minimum Flight: 2-4 weeks for optimization

CHANNEL SYNERGY EFFECTS

POSITIVE SYNERGY COMBINATIONS

TV Plus Search:
- Effect: TV drives brand search volume
- Search captures TV-generated demand
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks for search lift
- Recommendation: Budget 15-20% of TV spend in search

Social Plus Display:
- Effect: Social engagement builds retargeting pools
- Display retargets engaged social audiences
- Timeline: 4-6 weeks for pool maturity
- Recommendation: Coordinate audience segments

Email Plus Paid Media:
- Effect: Email nurtures paid media visitors
- Paid media reactivates email non-openers
- Timeline: Immediate coordination benefit
- Recommendation: Sync suppression lists

Content Plus Search:
- Effect: Content builds organic authority
- Search quality score improves
- Timeline: 8-12 weeks for SEO impact
- Recommendation: Align keyword strategy

Influencer Plus Paid Social:
- Effect: Creator content performs in paid
- Paid amplifies organic reach
- Timeline: Immediate amplification
- Recommendation: Negotiate content rights

Retail Plus Brand:
- Effect: Retail media captures demand
- Brand builds awareness for retail
- Timeline: 4-8 weeks for full funnel
- Recommendation: Coordinate messaging

NEGATIVE SYNERGY COMBINATIONS

Overlapping Retargeting:
- Issue: Frequency overload across channels
- Symptom: Declining engagement rates
- Fix: Coordinate frequency caps
- Maximum: 15-20 combined impressions weekly

Brand Search Cannibalization:
- Issue: Paid search captures organic traffic
- Symptom: High branded CPC with no lift
- Fix: Test brand search pausing
- Threshold: 70%+ organic coverage

Affiliate Conflict:
- Issue: Attribution conflicts with direct
- Symptom: Margin erosion on existing customers
- Fix: New customer only affiliate rules
- Policy: Clear attribution windows

Multiple DSP Conflict:
- Issue: Bidding against yourself
- Symptom: Rising CPMs same inventory
- Fix: Single DSP or deduplicated pools
- Approach: Unified audience management

Influencer Overlap:
- Issue: Same audiences multiple influencers
- Symptom: Diminishing incremental reach
- Fix: Audience analysis before selection
- Threshold: Less than 30% overlap

VERTICAL-SPECIFIC PLAYBOOKS

B2B CAMPAIGNS

Must-Have Channels:
- LINKEDIN: Decision maker targeting
- PAID_SEARCH: High intent capture
- EMAIL: Nurturing and ABM

Consider Adding:
- PODCAST_HOST_READ: Thought leadership
- CTV_STREAMING: Executive reach
- PROG_DISPLAY_PMP: Account targeting

Avoid or Limit:
- TIKTOK: Limited B2B audience
- SNAPCHAT: Consumer skew
- RETAIL_MEDIA: Not relevant

CPM Expectation: 2-3x consumer rates
Typical CPC: 5-15 LinkedIn, 3-8 search

ECOMMERCE CAMPAIGNS

Must-Have Channels:
- SHOPPING: Product visibility
- AMAZON_ADS: Marketplace presence
- META: Prospecting and retargeting

Consider Adding:
- PAID_SEARCH: Non-brand capture
- PROG_DISPLAY_PMP: Retargeting
- CTV_STREAMING: Brand building

Avoid or Limit:
- LINKEDIN: Unless B2B ecommerce
- PODCAST_HOST_READ: Long lead times

Key Metric: ROAS with full attribution
Typical Target: 4-8x ROAS blended

CPG AND RETAIL CAMPAIGNS

Must-Have Channels:
- WALMART_CONNECT: Grocery presence
- AMAZON_ADS: Marketplace share
- CTV_STREAMING: Household reach

Consider Adding:
- META: Awareness and engagement
- AUDIO_STREAMING: Incremental reach
- INSTACART: Delivery capture

Avoid or Limit:
- LINKEDIN: Consumer focus
- LOCAL_SEARCH: Unless regional

Focus: Household reach, purchase data
Attribution: In-store and online combined

DTC CAMPAIGNS

Must-Have Channels:
- META: Primary acquisition
- PAID_SEARCH: Intent capture
- EMAIL: Retention driver

Consider Adding:
- TIKTOK: Younger audiences
- INFLUENCER_PAID: Creator content
- YOUTUBE: Consideration content

Avoid or Limit:
- RETAIL_MEDIA: Direct only
- PROG_DISPLAY_OPEN: Quality concerns

Focus: CAC efficiency, LTV optimization
Key Metric: CAC to LTV ratio

Digital Social (META, TIKTOK): 3-5K monthly
Search (PAID_SEARCH, SHOPPING): 5-10K monthly
Programmatic Display: 10-15K monthly
CTV/OTT: 15-25K monthly
YouTube: 5-10K monthly
LinkedIn: 5-10K monthly
Podcast/Audio: 10-20K monthly
Retail Media: 3-10K monthly depending on platform
