SECTION 2 - KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS
================================================================================

SUBSCRIPTION METRICS

SUBSCRIBER COUNT
Total active subscribers.
Growth rate: Key health indicator.

SUBSCRIBER ACQUISITION COST (SAC)
Marketing cost to acquire one subscriber.
SAC = Marketing_Spend / New_Subscribers

TRIAL STARTS
Free trial initiations.
Trial to paid conversion: 40-70% typical.

CONVERSION RATE
Trial to paid conversion percentage.
Optimize through onboarding and content.

CHURN RATE
Percentage of subscribers lost per period.
Monthly churn: 3-7% typical for streaming.
Annual churn: 30-60% for streaming.

LIFETIME VALUE (LTV)
Expected revenue from subscriber relationship.
LTV = ARPU / Churn_Rate
Or: LTV = ARPU x Average_Subscriber_Lifespan

AVERAGE REVENUE PER USER (ARPU)
Monthly revenue per subscriber.
Track by tier and acquisition source.

LTV TO SAC RATIO
Target: 3x or higher.
Below 3x: Acquisition cost concern.

ENGAGEMENT METRICS

MONTHLY ACTIVE USERS (MAU)
Users engaging at least once per month.
Core engagement measure.

DAILY ACTIVE USERS (DAU)
Users engaging daily.
DAU/MAU ratio indicates stickiness.

WATCH TIME / LISTENING TIME
Total consumption hours.
Key content success metric.

CONTENT COMPLETION RATE
Percentage finishing content.
Series completion especially important.

SESSION FREQUENCY
How often users engage.
More frequency improves retention.

CONTENT PERFORMANCE METRICS

CONTENT EFFICIENCY
Cost to produce vs engagement generated.
Informs content investment.

CATALOG UTILIZATION
Percentage of catalog consumed.
Long-tail engagement valuable.

NEW CONTENT IMPACT
Engagement lift from new releases.
Informs release strategy.

ROAS CALCULATION

STREAMING ROAS
ROAS = (Subscriber_Revenue - Marketing_Spend) / Marketing_Spend

Where: Subscriber_Revenue = New_Subscribers x LTV

EXAMPLE
- Marketing spend: $1,000,000
- New subscribers: 50,000
- LTV: $80
- Revenue: 50,000 x $80 = $4,000,000
- ROAS = ($4,000,000 - $1,000,000) / $1,000,000 = 3.0

For transactional (film, events):
ROAS = (Ticket_Revenue - Marketing_Spend) / Marketing_Spend

PLANNING RECOMMENDATIONS

BUDGET ALLOCATION FRAMEWORK
- Tentpole campaigns: 30-50% of annual
- Always-on acquisition: 30-40%
- Retention and engagement: 15-25%
- Brand building: 10-20%

CHANNEL PRIORITIZATION
1. Video advertising (category essential)
2. Social media (engagement and awareness)
3. CTV (cord-cutter reach)
4. Influencer (authenticity)
5. Performance channels (efficiency)

OPTIMIZATION PRIORITIES

ACQUISITION OPTIMIZATION
- Reduce SAC while maintaining quality
- Improve trial-to-paid conversion
- Test creative and offers continuously

RETENTION OPTIMIZATION
- Identify churn predictors
- Intervene before cancellation
- Personalize recommendations
- Re-engage inactive users

SEGMENT-SPECIFIC GUIDANCE

STREAMING SERVICES
- Content slate drives marketing timing
- Retention equally important as acquisition
- Bundle partnerships for distribution
- International expansion opportunities

FILM RELEASES
- Compressed, high-intensity campaigns
- Opening weekend critical
- Reviews and word-of-mouth important
- Platform strategy (theatrical, streaming, hybrid)

GAMING
- Community and influencer essential
- Live service requires ongoing engagement
- Seasonal events and updates
- Platform-specific strategies

WARNING SIGNS
- SAC rising without LTV increase
- Churn rate increasing
- Trial conversion declining
- Engagement metrics falling
- Content fatigue indicators
