============================================================
PURPOSE
============================================================

This document provides industry-specific guidance for Financial Services media planning. It supplements base agent knowledge with financial services-specific considerations including regulatory compliance, product restrictions, trust messaging, and lead quality prioritization.

============================================================
FINANCIAL SERVICES MARKET CHARACTERISTICS
============================================================

REGULATORY ENVIRONMENT

Financial marketing operates under extensive oversight:

- SEC regulates investment product advertising
- FINRA oversees broker-dealer communications
- CFPB enforces consumer financial protection
- State regulators add jurisdiction-specific requirements
- OCC and Federal Reserve for banking institutions

PRODUCT CATEGORIES

Financial services encompasses diverse offerings:

BANKING AND DEPOSITS
- Checking and savings accounts
- Certificates of deposit
- Money market accounts
- High-yield savings products

LENDING PRODUCTS
- Mortgages and home equity
- Personal loans and lines of credit
- Credit cards and charge cards
- Auto and specialty lending

INVESTMENT PRODUCTS
- Brokerage accounts
- Retirement accounts (IRA, 401k)
- Mutual funds and ETFs
- Advisory and managed services

INSURANCE PRODUCTS
- Life and health insurance
- Property and casualty
- Annuities and retirement income
- Supplemental coverage

AUDIENCE CHARACTERISTICS

Financial services customers exhibit specific patterns:

- Major financial decisions involve research periods of weeks to months
- Trust and security rank as top decision factors
- Multiple household members often involved in decisions
- Life events trigger financial product consideration
- Price sensitivity balanced against reputation and service

============================================================
COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
============================================================

ADVERTISING STANDARDS

Financial advertising must meet regulatory standards:

DISCLOSURE REQUIREMENTS
- APR and fee disclosure for credit products
- Past performance disclaimers for investments
- FDIC and SIPC membership statements
- Licensing and registration disclosures
- Fair lending and equal housing notices

PROHIBITED PRACTICES
- False or misleading statements
- Omission of material information
- Guarantees of investment performance
- Deceptive fee or rate presentation
- Testimonials without proper disclosure

REVIEW AND APPROVAL

Financial advertising requires compliance review:

- Pre-approval for regulated communications
- Documentation of claims and support
- Archival requirements for advertisements
- Periodic review and audit processes
- Escalation procedures for violations

PLATFORM RESTRICTIONS

Each platform restricts financial advertising:

GOOGLE ADS
- Certification required for financial services
- Country-specific restrictions apply
- Cryptocurrency and speculative products limited
- Personal loans face additional requirements

META PLATFORMS
- Credit advertising subject to Special Ad Category
- Housing and employment restrictions overlap
- Targeting limitations for credit products
- Disclaimer requirements for offers

PROGRAMMATIC
- Publisher and exchange policies vary
- Financial sector brand safety considerations
- Data targeting compliance requirements
- Transparency and consent documentation

============================================================
TRUST AND SECURITY MESSAGING
============================================================

BRAND TRUST FOUNDATIONS

Build trust through consistent messaging:

SECURITY SIGNALS
- Encryption and data protection messaging
- Fraud protection and monitoring features
- Insurance and guarantee coverage
- Regulatory compliance and oversight

STABILITY INDICATORS
- Years in business and company history
- Assets under management or deposits
- Customer base size and growth
- Industry awards and recognition

SERVICE EXCELLENCE
- Customer service availability and quality
- Digital experience and convenience
- Problem resolution commitment
- Personal relationship emphasis

CREATIVE STRATEGY FOR TRUST

Execute trust-building through creative approach:

- Use real customer testimonials with proper disclosure
- Feature recognizable spokespersons carefully
- Show physical branch locations where relevant
- Demonstrate digital security features
- Include regulatory and membership logos

============================================================
LEAD QUALITY PRIORITIZATION
============================================================

QUALITY OVER VOLUME APPROACH

Financial services requires qualified leads:

LEAD QUALIFICATION CRITERIA
- Credit score and financial profile alignment
- Income and employment verification signals
- Geographic and regulatory eligibility
- Intent and timeline indicators
- Product-specific qualification factors

QUALITY INDICATORS BY PRODUCT

Different products require different qualification:

MORTGAGE LEADS
- Home purchase versus refinance intent
- Price range and down payment readiness
- Employment and income stability
- Geographic location and market
- Timeline and urgency indicators

CREDIT CARD LEADS
- Credit tier and score range
- Spend and payment patterns
- Rewards preference alignment
- Existing product holdings
- Risk profile indicators

INVESTMENT LEADS
- Investable asset level
- Risk tolerance and objectives
- Self-directed versus advised preference
- Account type needs
- Time horizon and goals

COST PER QUALIFIED LEAD

Benchmark expectations for financial leads:

- Mortgage leads: 30 to 150 USD depending on qualification
- Credit card leads: 20 to 80 USD for qualified applicants
- Personal loan leads: 25 to 100 USD for qualified borrowers
- Investment leads: 50 to 200 USD for qualified prospects
- Insurance leads: 15 to 75 USD depending on product

PRODUCT-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES
============================================================

CREDIT CARD MARKETING

Credit card acquisition considerations:

- Rewards and benefits messaging drives consideration
- Sign-up bonus offers create urgency
- APR and fee transparency builds trust
- Approval odds messaging manages expectations
- Ongoing engagement maintains card usage

MORTGAGE MARKETING

Mortgage marketing considerations:

- Rate messaging requires careful compliance
- Local market presence important for trust
- Life event targeting drives relevance
- Process simplicity differentiates
- Long consideration cycle requires patience

INVESTMENT MARKETING

Investment product considerations:

- Performance claims require disclosure
- Risk-appropriate messaging by product
- Educational content builds confidence
- Digital experience increasingly important
- Fee transparency growing in importance

============================================================
FINANCIAL SERVICES MEASUREMENT
============================================================

ACQUISITION METRICS

Track customer acquisition effectiveness:

- Cost per application and per funded account
- Application-to-approval conversion rate
- Funding rate for approved applications
- Time from application to funding
- Initial deposit or balance amounts

LIFETIME VALUE FOCUS

Financial services measurement emphasizes LTV:

- First-year revenue by acquisition source
- Retention rate by cohort and channel
- Cross-sell and product expansion
- Balance and activity growth over time
- Lifetime profitability by segment

ATTRIBUTION CONSIDERATIONS

Financial services attribution challenges:

- Long consideration cycles require extended windows
- Multiple touchpoints across devices and channels
- Offline conversion common for many products
- Privacy regulations limit tracking ability
- Branch and call center attribution complexity

============================================================
CHANNEL STRATEGY
============================================================

CHANNEL MIX RECOMMENDATIONS

Financial services typical channel allocation:

AWARENESS CHANNELS
- Television and streaming for brand building
- Digital display for reach extension
- Social for brand presence and engagement
- Sponsorships for trust and association

CONSIDERATION CHANNELS
- Search for intent capture
- Content marketing for education
- Email for nurturing and offers
- Retargeting for engaged prospects

CONVERSION CHANNELS
- Paid search brand and product terms
- Direct response display and social
- Email for qualified prospects
- Affiliate for performance-based reach

CHANNEL-SPECIFIC GUIDANCE

Platform considerations for financial services:

- Search essential for high-intent capture
- Display requires careful brand safety controls
- Social faces targeting limitations for credit
- Connected TV growing for brand and reach
- Direct mail remains effective for many products

============================================================
COMMON FINANCIAL PLANNING MISTAKES
============================================================

AVOID THESE PATTERNS

Frequent errors in financial services media planning include:

- Prioritizing lead volume over qualification
- Inadequate compliance review processes
- Making performance claims without proper disclosure
- Ignoring platform-specific policy requirements
- Using broad targeting for regulated products
- Underestimating consideration cycle length
- Neglecting trust signals in creative
- Missing cross-sell and retention opportunities
